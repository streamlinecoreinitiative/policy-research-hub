# Mangrove Restoration and Blue Carbon Finance in the Sahel: Enhancing Resilience for Communities

## Executive Summary

The Sahel region, spanning across several countries in West Africa, faces significant environmental challenges due to climate change. Rising temperatures, changing precipitation patterns, and increased frequency of droughts threaten the livelihoods of millions who depend on agriculture, livestock, and natural resources. This brief highlights the importance of mangrove restoration and blue carbon finance as a key strategy for enhancing resilience in the Sahel region.
