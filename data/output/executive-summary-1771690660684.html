Policy Recommendations

To address these challenges, we recommend:

1. Enhanced Water Resources Management: Invest in infrastructure projects such as water harvesting and purification systems to improve access to safe drinking water. Implement community-based watershed management initiatives led by indigenous councils.

  1. Increased Clean Energy Access: Promote the adoption of renewable energy sources like solar power through subsidies, grants, and public-private partnerships. Increase electrification rates in rural areas using off-grid solutions such as mini-grids.
  2. Climate Adaptation Strategies: Develop climate-resilient agricultural practices, improve early warning systems for extreme weather events, and invest in disaster preparedness and response mechanisms.
