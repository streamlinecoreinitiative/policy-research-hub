   - Implement community-led initiatives for rainwater harvesting.
   - Promote water-efficient technologies.
2. **Accelerated Renewable Energy Deployment**:
   - Increase investment in solar power generation and other renewable energy sources.
   - Encourage private sector participation through public-private partnerships (PPP).
3. **Climate Resilience Programs**:
   - Develop early warning systems for climate-related disasters.
   - Implement disaster preparedness plans.
   - Fund research into climate-resilient crops and agricultural practices.
