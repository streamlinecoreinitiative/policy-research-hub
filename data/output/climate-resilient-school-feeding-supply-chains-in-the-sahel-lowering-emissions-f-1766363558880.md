### Executive Summary

The Sahel region faces severe climate-related challenges that threaten food security, water availability, and economic stability. The school feeding sector is particularly vulnerable to these impacts. This brief proposes an integrated approach to address environmental resilience in lower-income countries by combining water security, clean energy, and climate adaptation strategies.
