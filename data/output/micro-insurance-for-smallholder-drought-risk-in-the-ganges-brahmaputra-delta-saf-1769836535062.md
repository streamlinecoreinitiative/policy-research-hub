## Policy Recommendations

### 1. Microgrids for Rural Clinics

Develop microgrid systems that integrate solar panels with battery storage to power clinics in remote areas.

* Benefits:
	+ Ensure continuous medical services
	+ Reduce healthcare costs by decreasing reliance on diesel generators
	+ Promote local employment
* Action Steps:
	+ Conduct feasibility studies in selected regions (e.g., Kenya, Tanzania)
	+ Secure funding through international aid and local investments (e.g., World Bank, USAID)
	+ Train local technicians for maintenance

### 2. Solar Cold-Chains for Fisheries

Deploy solar-powered cold-chain solutions to preserve fish during transportation from fishing grounds to markets.

* Benefits:
	+ Extend shelf life
	+ Reduce post-harvest losses
	+ Enhance market access
	+ Improve nutrition by ensuring fresh fish availability
* Action Steps:
	+ Identify pilot sites with high potential for adoption (e.g., Ghana, Senegal)
	+ Collaborate with local fisheries associations for implementation
	+ Monitor impact on market prices and consumer health

### 3. Watershed Co-Management with Indigenous Councils

Engage indigenous communities in managing watershed resources, leveraging their traditional knowledge and practices.

* Benefits:
	+ Enhance ecological sustainability
	+ Foster social cohesion
	+ Ensure equitable resource use
* Action Steps:
	+ Establish partnerships between local governments and indigenous groups (e.g., Kenya, Tanzania)
	+ Develop joint management plans focusing on water conservation and climate adaptation strategies
