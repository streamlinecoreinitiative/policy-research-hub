# Agroforestry Value Chains for Cash Crops in the Central America Dry Corridor: Lowering Emissions from Critical Sectors

## Executive Summary

The Central America dry corridor is highly vulnerable to climate change, with severe water scarcity and droughts affecting agricultural productivity and food security. This brief presents an integrated approach to address these challenges through agroforestry value chains for cash crops.

## Introduction to Water Scarcity

Climate change exacerbates water scarcity in the Central America dry corridor, impacting agriculture and food security.
