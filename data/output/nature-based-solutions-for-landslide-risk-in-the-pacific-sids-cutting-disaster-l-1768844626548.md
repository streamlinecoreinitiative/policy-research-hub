## Executive Summary

This policy brief recommends that governments of Pacific Small Island Developing States (SIDS) invest in nature-based solutions to mitigate landslide risk, which poses a significant threat to these countries' development. Specifically, we recommend:

* **Develop and implement policies promoting the adoption of green infrastructure**, such as terracing, contour planting, and afforestation, to reduce soil erosion and landslides.
* **Establish incentives for public and private sector investments** in nature-based solutions, including financial support and tax breaks.

By investing in these nature-based solutions, governments can reduce disaster losses, protect vulnerable communities, and promote sustainable development.
