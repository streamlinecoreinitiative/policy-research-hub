## Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency

Micro-hydro technology involves the use of small-scale hydroelectric systems to generate electricity. This method is energy-efficient and can significantly reduce reliance on diesel generators.

### Advantages of Micro-Hydro:

*   Reduced greenhouse gas emissions
*   Increased energy efficiency
*   Improved water security
