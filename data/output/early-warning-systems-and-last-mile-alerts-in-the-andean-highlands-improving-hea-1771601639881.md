## Policy Recommendations

1.  **Investment in Water Infrastructure**: Develop and finance boreholes, rainwater harvesting ponds, and other water supply systems in rural areas.
2.  **Promote Renewable Energy**: Encourage the use of solar power through subsidies, grants, and tax incentives for households and businesses.
3.  **Implement Climate Adaptation Measures**: Support projects like afforestation initiatives, early warning systems, and improved drainage networks to mitigate climate risks.

## Implementation Considerations

Initial investments in water infrastructure can range from $100-$500 per household depending on the technology used; renewable energy systems may cost more but could be offset by government grants. Collaboration between governments, NGOs, and international organizations is essential for successful implementation.
