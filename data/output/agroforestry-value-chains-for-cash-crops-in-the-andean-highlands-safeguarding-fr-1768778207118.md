# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Andean Highlands

## Executive Summary

The Andean highlands face severe water scarcity challenges due to historical droughts and limited access to reliable irrigation systems, exacerbating economic instability caused by reduced crop yields. This brief explores the potential of agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems as a solution for climate-resilient irrigation in this region.

## Challenges and Current Practices

### Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Andean Highlands

The Andean highlands have experienced droughts, leading to severe water scarcity challenges. Traditional irrigation methods like surface water and rain-fed systems are limited in efficiency and sustainability.

### Current Challenges Faced by Farmers and Communities

Farmers in the Andean highlands face significant challenges due to water scarcity, including reduced crop yields, financial instability, and limited access to reliable irrigation systems.
