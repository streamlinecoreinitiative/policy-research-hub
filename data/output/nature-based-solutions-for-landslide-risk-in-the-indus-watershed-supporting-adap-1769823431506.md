1. **Enhance Water Security**: Implement water scarcity management strategies such as:

	* Desalination plants
	* Wastewater reuse technologies (World Bank)

	* Promote decentralized renewable energy solutions like solar home systems and mini-grids for off-grid areas
	* Encourage investment in large-scale wind and solar farms, focusing on geographically suitable sites
3. **Strengthen Climate Resilience**:
	* Develop early warning systems for climate-related disasters to enable timely response measures
