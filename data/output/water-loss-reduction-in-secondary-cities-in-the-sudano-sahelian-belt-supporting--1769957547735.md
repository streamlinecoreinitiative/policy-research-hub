**Policy Recommendations**

1. **Water Security**:
	* Introduce community-led water management systems such as rainwater harvesting and borehole drilling in rural areas.
	* Increase investment in water treatment facilities to ensure safe drinking water supply.
2. **Clean Energy**:
	* Invest in renewable energy projects like solar, wind, and hydroelectric power generation.
	* Promote micro-grids and decentralized renewable energy solutions for off-grid communities.
3. **Climate Adaptation**:
	* Implement early warning systems for climate-related disasters to minimize losses.
	* Encourage the adoption of drought-resistant crops and improved farming techniques.
