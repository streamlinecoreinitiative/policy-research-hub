## Policy Recommendations

1. **Improve Water Security:**
	* Invest in water harvesting technologies such as rainwater collection systems, groundwater extraction projects, and desalination plants.
	* Implement community-led initiatives to ensure equitable access.
2. **Enhance Clean Energy Access:**
	* Increase investment in renewable energy sources like solar, wind, and hydroelectric power.
	* Promote decentralized renewable energy solutions such as mini-grids and off-grid systems that can be installed at the household or village level.
3. **Foster Climate Resilience:**
	* Develop early warning systems for droughts and heatwaves using technology like satellite imagery and IoT devices to provide timely information.
	* Create climate-resilient infrastructure, including flood control structures and water storage facilities.
