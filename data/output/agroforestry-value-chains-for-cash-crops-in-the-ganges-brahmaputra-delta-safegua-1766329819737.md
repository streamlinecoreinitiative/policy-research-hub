The Ethiopian Highlands face severe water scarcity due to climate change impacts. Erratic rainfall patterns have become more frequent, characterized by variable annual rainfall (1). Traditional irrigation systems are energy-intensive and costly, resulting in significant water losses through evaporation and seepage.
