## Environmental Resilience in Lower-Income Countries

### Water Security

* Climate change has severe impacts on agriculture, including erratic rainfall patterns and droughts.
* Resilient irrigation methods are crucial for food security and economic stability in vulnerable regions.
* Agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems can provide efficient water supply solutions.

### Clean Energy

* Micro-hydro technology offers reduced reliance on diesel generators and increased energy efficiency.
* Integration with agroforestry practices enhances carbon sequestration, but evidence is needed to quantify the reduction in greenhouse gas emissions.

### Climate Adaptation

* Mangrove restoration in the Indus watershed can help mitigate climate-related disasters, such as floods and saltwater intrusion.
* Blue carbon finance mechanisms can support sustainable livelihoods for local communities while promoting environmental conservation.
