## Context & Challenge

Sub-Saharan Africa is the most vulnerable region in terms of access to clean water and electricity (World Bank Open Data). Only 32.4% of people have safe drinking water (World Bank Open Data), and 53.3% access electricity (World Bank Open Data). Climate change intensifies these challenges by increasing droughts and floods, leading to reduced agricultural productivity and health risks.
