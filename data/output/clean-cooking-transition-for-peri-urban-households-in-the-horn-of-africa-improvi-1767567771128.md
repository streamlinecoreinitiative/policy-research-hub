# Clean Cooking Transition for Peri-Urban Households in the Horn of Africa: Improving Health Outcomes in Heatwaves

## Executive Summary

The Horn of Africa faces significant challenges due to severe droughts and heatwaves, affecting peri-urban households. The transition to clean cooking practices is crucial for improving health outcomes during extreme weather events.

## Environmental Resilience in Peri-Urban Households

### Water Scarcity and Climate Change Impacts

*   The Horn of Africa has a semi-arid climate with erratic rainfall patterns.
*   Climate change exacerbates water scarcity, affecting agriculture, livestock, and human consumption.
*   Peri-urban households rely on boreholes or canals for irrigation and drinking water.

### Current Irrigation Practices and Challenges

*   Existing irrigation systems suffer from high evaporation rates.
*   Water loss through seepage is significant due to inadequate infrastructure.
*   Crop yields are reduced, impacting food security and economic stability.
