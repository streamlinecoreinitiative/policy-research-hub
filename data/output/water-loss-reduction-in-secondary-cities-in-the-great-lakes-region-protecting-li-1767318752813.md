## Clean Energy and Climate Adaptation: Micro-Hydro Systems for Efficiency

### Overview of Micro-Hydro Technology

Micro-hydro technology involves the use of small-scale hydroelectric systems to generate clean energy. This can be particularly effective in rural areas where access to electricity is limited.

* Benefits of micro-hydro:
	+ Reduced reliance on diesel generators
	+ Increased energy efficiency and reduced emissions
