## Executive Summary

The Central African Rainforest faces severe water scarcity due to climate change, affecting agriculture and local communities. This brief proposes integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems as a sustainable solution that enhances both water security and renewable energy use.

## Introduction to Water Scarcity

Climate change affects agriculture in the Central African Rainforest through erratic rainfall patterns leading to reduced water availability for crops. Increased evaporation from wetlands exacerbates water scarcity issues.
