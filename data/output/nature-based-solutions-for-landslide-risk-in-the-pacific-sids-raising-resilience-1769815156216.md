## Policy Recommendations

**Enhancing Access to Safe Drinking Water**

1. **Invest in Desalination Plants**: Establish desalination facilities in coastal areas for safe drinking water supply.
2. **Promote Rainwater Harvesting**: Implement rainwater harvesting systems in rural and urban settings, supported by government subsidies and community engagement.

**Increasing Renewable Energy Consumption**

3. **Develop Solar-Powered Wells**: Install solar-powered wells to increase access to clean water in remote communities.
4. **Support Grid Extension Projects**: Fund grid extension projects to connect more households to electricity grids, with a focus on rural areas.
5. **Invest in Microgrids**: Implement microgrid systems for off-grid communities and small businesses.

**Building Climate Resilience**

6. **Implement Early Warning Systems**: Deploy early warning systems for climate-related hazards like droughts and floods.
7. **Enhance Water Loss Reduction**: Introduce measures to reduce water loss from distribution networks, focusing on secondary cities.
8. **Promote Nature-Based Solutions**: Implement agroforestry value chains in the Andean highlands to improve watershed management.
