## Policy Recommendations

To address the challenges of water security and climate resilience, specific policy recommendations include:

1. **Expand Solar-Powered Irrigation Systems**: Implement pilot projects in drought-prone areas to demonstrate the feasibility and cost-effectiveness of solar-powered irrigation systems.
2. **Promote Community-Based Watershed Management**: Establish community-led watershed management initiatives that integrate renewable energy sources for water harvesting and purification, ensuring long-term sustainability.
3. **Invest in Resilient Hydropower Plants**: Develop hydropower plants with adaptive features to withstand climate-induced changes such as increased frequency of droughts or floods.

## Implementation Considerations

Implementing these recommendations requires coordinated efforts among government agencies, NGOs, and international partners. Key considerations include:

- **Funding Mechanisms**: Establish dedicated funding mechanisms for renewable energy projects and water infrastructure improvements.
- **Public Awareness Campaigns**: Launch public awareness campaigns to educate communities on the benefits of adopting climate-resilient practices.
