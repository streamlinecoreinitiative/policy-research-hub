# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Ethiopian Highlands

## Executive Summary

Climate change exacerbates water scarcity in the Ethiopian Highlands, impacting agriculture and food security. Traditional irrigation methods are energy-intensive and costly, while agroforestry improves soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration. This brief proposes integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems for climate-resilient irrigation.

### Introduction to Water Scarcity
