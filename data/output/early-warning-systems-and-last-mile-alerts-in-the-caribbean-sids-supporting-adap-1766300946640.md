# Early-Warning Systems and Last-Mile Alerts in the Caribbean SIDS: Supporting Adaptation Targets in NDCs

## Executive Summary

Climate change poses significant challenges to Small Island Developing States (SIDS) in the Caribbean, particularly in terms of water security, clean energy, and climate adaptation. This brief proposes the implementation of early-warning systems and last-mile alerts as a critical component of national adaptation strategies outlined in Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs). By leveraging cutting-edge technology and building on existing initiatives, Caribbean SIDS can enhance their resilience to climate-related hazards while promoting sustainable development.
