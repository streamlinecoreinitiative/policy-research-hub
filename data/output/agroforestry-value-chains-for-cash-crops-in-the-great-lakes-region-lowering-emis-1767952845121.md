# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Central African Rainforest

## Executive Summary

Climate change and water scarcity pose significant challenges to agricultural productivity and food security in lower-income countries, particularly in regions like the Central African Rainforest. This brief highlights the potential of integrated agroforestry and micro-hydro systems to address these issues.
