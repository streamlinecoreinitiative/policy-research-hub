### Policy Recommendations

To address these challenges, we recommend the following specific policies:

1. **Expand Access to Safe Drinking Water**:
   * Invest in infrastructure development and maintenance to ensure reliable access to safe drinking water.
   * Implement community-based management systems that empower local populations to maintain water sources.
   * Support research and innovation for low-cost, sustainable water treatment technologies suitable for rural areas.

2. **Enhance Renewable Energy Adoption**:
   * Increase public-private partnerships to finance renewable energy projects such as solar photovoltaic (PV) systems and small hydropower plants.
   * Develop supportive policies that incentivize the use of renewables, including feed-in tariffs and tax exemptions.
   * Promote decentralized renewable energy solutions like microgrids and off-grid systems to serve remote communities.

3. **Strengthen Climate Resilience**:
   * Integrate climate-resilient infrastructure design principles into new and existing water supply and power generation projects.
   * Implement early warning systems for extreme weather events to facilitate timely responses.
   * Establish climate adaptation funds that prioritize investments in resilient, sustainable infrastructure such as flood barriers and irrigation systems.

4. **Promote Energy Efficiency and Conservation**:
   * Encourage energy-efficient appliances and lighting through subsidies or incentives.
   * Develop education campaigns about water conservation practices among households and businesses.
   * Support research into innovative cooling technologies to mitigate the impact of heatwaves on electricity demand.
