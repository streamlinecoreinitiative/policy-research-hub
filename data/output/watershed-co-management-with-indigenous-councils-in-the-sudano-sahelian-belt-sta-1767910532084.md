# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Ethiopian Highlands

## Executive Summary

Climate change has significantly impacted agriculture in the Ethiopian Highlands by causing erratic rainfall patterns, leading to severe water scarcity and affecting food security. Integrated agroforestry and micro-hydro systems offer a sustainable solution. This brief outlines the benefits of agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems for climate-resilient irrigation.
