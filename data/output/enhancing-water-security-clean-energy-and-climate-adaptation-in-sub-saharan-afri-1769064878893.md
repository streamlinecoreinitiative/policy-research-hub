### Executive Summary

To address these challenges, we recommend the following policy interventions:

1. **Infrastructure Development**: Invest in water infrastructure projects such as desalination plants and improved sanitation facilities to increase access to safely managed drinking water.
2. **Renewable Energy Promotion**: Increase investment in solar, wind, and hydroelectric power generation to meet electricity demand sustainably.
3. **Climate Adaptation Strategies**: Develop early-warning systems and last-mile alerts for climate-related disasters such as floods and droughts.
