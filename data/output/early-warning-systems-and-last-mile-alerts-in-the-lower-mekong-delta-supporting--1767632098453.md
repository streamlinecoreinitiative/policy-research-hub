**Section 4: Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency**

Micro-hydro technology enables efficient use of small-scale hydroelectric systems to power irrigation pumps, reducing reliance on diesel generators and energy costs. By harnessing local water resources, farmers can maintain a stable water supply while minimizing environmental impacts.
