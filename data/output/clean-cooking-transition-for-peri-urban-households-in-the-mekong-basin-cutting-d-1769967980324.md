### Policy Recommendations

1. **Expand Water Infrastructure**: Invest $3 billion over the next three years to increase access to safely managed drinking water, targeting rural areas with specific funding allocations.
	* Develop and implement sustainable water management practices in agriculture and industry.
	* Establish a regional water fund to support community-led initiatives for water conservation and management.
2. **Accelerate Renewable Energy Deployment**: Allocate $4 billion over the next five years to promote renewable energy technologies, including solar and wind power.
	* Develop policies that incentivize renewable energy deployment, such as tax breaks and feed-in tariffs.
	* Prioritize grid connectivity in underserved regions to ensure equitable access to clean energy.
3. **Climate Adaptation Programs**: Invest $3 billion over the next five years to implement early-warning systems and climate-resilient infrastructure investments.
	* Develop and disseminate climate information services for farmers, fishermen, and other vulnerable groups.
	* Support community-led initiatives for climate change adaptation and resilience building.
