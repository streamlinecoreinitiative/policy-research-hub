1. Expand Access to Safe Drinking Water: * Implement water sanitation and hygiene (WASH) programs in schools and communities. * Invest in community-based water supply systems like boreholes and rainwater harvesting ponds. * Encourage private sector partnerships for cost-effective solutions.

  • Increase Electricity Access through Renewable Energy Solutions:

  • Enhance Climate Resilience through Adaptation Measures:
* Develop early warning systems for droughts and floods using satellite imagery and IoT sensors. * Promote climate-smart agriculture practices such as drought-resistant crop varieties and water-efficient irrigation techniques. * Build resilient infrastructure like flood barriers and drought-resistant housing materials.
