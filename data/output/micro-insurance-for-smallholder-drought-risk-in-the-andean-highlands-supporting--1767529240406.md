Climate change has significantly impacted agriculture in the Andean highlands, leading to erratic rainfall patterns and increased water scarcity. This has severe consequences for food security and economic stability, particularly among smallholder farmers who rely heavily on irrigation systems.

* Water scarcity is a critical issue in the Andean highlands, exacerbating poverty and food insecurity.
* Resilient irrigation methods are essential for sustainable agriculture and community development.
