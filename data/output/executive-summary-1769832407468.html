Policy Recommendations

To address water security, electricity access, and climate adaptation, government officials and development practitioners should prioritize the following:

1. Water Security: Invest in improved sanitation facilities and clean water infrastructure such as rainwater harvesting systems and boreholes. Implement cost-sharing schemes for communities to encourage participation.

  • Clean Energy: Promote solar-powered energy solutions for households and small businesses. Develop microgrids that integrate renewable energy sources like solar, wind, and hydroelectric power. Provide subsidies and training programs to support the adoption of these technologies.
  • Climate Adaptation: Establish early warning systems for climate-related disasters such as floods and droughts. Encourage water-harvesting practices and soil conservation measures to enhance resilience against climate change impacts.
