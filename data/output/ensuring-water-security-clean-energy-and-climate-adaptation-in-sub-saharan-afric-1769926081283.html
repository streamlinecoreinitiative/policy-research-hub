Ensuring Water Security, Clean Energy, and Climate Adaptation in Sub-Saharan Africa

Executive Summary

Sub-Saharan Africa faces significant challenges related to water security, clean energy access, and climate resilience. To address these issues, we recommend increasing funding for water supply projects targeting rural areas, investing in rainwater harvesting systems, and promoting small-scale irrigation schemes and community-led initiatives. Additionally, implementing feed-in tariffs and renewable energy subsidies can enhance the deployment of solar, wind, and hydroelectric projects. Furthermore, investing in early warning systems for droughts and floods, promoting climate-resilient agricultural practices, and building community resilience through disaster risk reduction programs are crucial for climate adaptation.
