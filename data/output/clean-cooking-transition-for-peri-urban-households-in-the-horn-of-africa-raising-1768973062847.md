## Policy Recommendations

1. **Solar-Powered Wells in Rural Areas**:
	* Implement solar-powered wells to improve access to clean drinking water in rural regions. These systems are cost-effective and sustainable, reducing reliance on traditional energy sources.
2. **Enhanced Watershed Co-management with Indigenous Councils**:
	* Collaborate with indigenous communities for climate-resilient watershed management practices. This approach leverages local knowledge and fosters long-term sustainability.
3. **Microgrids for Rural Clinics**:
	* Develop microgrid systems to provide reliable electricity in rural health facilities, ensuring continuous power supply for essential services like refrigeration of medicines.

## Implementation Considerations

* **Costs**: Initial investments may range from $100-$500 per well (cost estimates vary by region) and $200-$1000 for microgrids.
* **Barriers**: Limited funding, technical expertise, and community buy-in are potential obstacles. Collaboration with international partners can help overcome these challenges.
