**Nature-Based Solutions for Landslide Risk in the Lower Mekong Delta: Enhancing Water Security and Renewable Energy Use**

Executive Summary:

The Lower Mekong delta is highly vulnerable to climate change, facing severe water scarcity and increased landslide risk due to droughts and floods. This brief explores the potential of nature-based solutions, specifically agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems, to enhance both water security and renewable energy use in this region.

**I. Introduction to Water Scarcity and Climate Change Impacts**

The Lower Mekong delta is exposed to erratic rainfall patterns and increased evaporation from wetlands, exacerbating water scarcity issues. These impacts are further complicated by economic challenges such as high labor costs for constructing and maintaining irrigation systems.
