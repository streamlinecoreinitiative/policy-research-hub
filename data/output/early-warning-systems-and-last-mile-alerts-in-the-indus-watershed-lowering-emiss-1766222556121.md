## Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency

Micro-hydro technology offers a sustainable solution for irrigation, reducing reliance on diesel generators and increasing energy efficiency. By harnessing the power of small-scale hydroelectric systems, farmers can access clean energy while minimizing their carbon footprint.

### Key Features:

- Reduced reliance on diesel generators.
- Increased energy efficiency through the use of hydroelectric power.
- Potential for community-based ownership and management.

## Integration of Agroforestry and Micro-Hydro in Climate-Resilient Water Supply

Pilot projects have demonstrated the effectiveness of integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems. These integrated solutions offer a cost-effective approach to climate-resilient irrigation, enhancing both water security and renewable energy use.

### Key Outcomes:
