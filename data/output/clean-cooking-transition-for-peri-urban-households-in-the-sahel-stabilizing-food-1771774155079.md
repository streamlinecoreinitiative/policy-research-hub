### Policy Recommendations

1. **Enhance Water Security**:
	* Implement large-scale water harvesting and storage projects in arid regions.
	* Promote community-led initiatives like rainwater harvesting ponds and small-scale irrigation systems.
	* Invest in wastewater treatment facilities and improve sanitation infrastructure to enhance safe drinking water access.
2. **Promote Clean Energy Solutions**:
	* Expand solar energy deployment, particularly through solar-powered wells and micro-grids for remote communities.
	* Encourage off-grid renewable energy solutions such as solar home systems and mini-grids.
	* Invest in public-private partnerships (PPPs) to finance renewable energy projects.
3. **Build Climate Resilience**:
	* Develop early warning systems, focusing on the Andean highlands where climate stress is particularly acute.
	* Implement agroforestry value chains for cash crops to enhance resilience against weather shocks.
	* Strengthen institutional frameworks and governance structures to manage natural resources sustainably.

### Implementation Considerations

* **Costs**: Initial investments in infrastructure projects can range from $10 million to over $50 million, depending on the scale of the project. However, long-term benefits include improved health outcomes, economic growth, and reduced poverty.
* **Barriers**: Political inertia, lack of funding, and technical challenges are common barriers. Addressing these requires strong political commitment, innovative financing mechanisms, and robust implementation plans.
