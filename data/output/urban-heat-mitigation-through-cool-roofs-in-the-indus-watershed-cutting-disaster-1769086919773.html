    1. Expand Renewable Energy Sources:

3. Foster Sustainable Development Practices: - Encourage agroforestry value chains for cash crops that integrate climate-smart irrigation techniques (World Bank Open Data, 2024). - Support microgrids in rural clinics and community centers to ensure reliable electricity supply (World Bank, 2023).
