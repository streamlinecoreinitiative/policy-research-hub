## Micro-Hydro Systems for Efficiency

Micro-hydro technology is a small-scale hydroelectric system that reduces reliance on diesel generators and increases energy efficiency in irrigation. This approach can be particularly beneficial in regions with abundant water resources but limited access to electricity.

### Benefits of Micro-Hydro:
