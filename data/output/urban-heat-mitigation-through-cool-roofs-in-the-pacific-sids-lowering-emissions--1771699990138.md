## Context & Challenge

Sub-Saharan Africa is one of the most vulnerable regions globally to climate change impacts like droughts, floods, and extreme weather events (World Bank Open Data). The region's high population density and limited infrastructure exacerbate these challenges. Ensuring water security, clean energy access, and climate resilience are essential for sustainable development in this region.
