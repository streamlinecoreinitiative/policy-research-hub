# Water Loss Reduction in Secondary Cities in the Mekong Basin: Safeguarding Freshwater Ecosystems

## Executive Summary

Water scarcity poses significant challenges for lower-income countries, particularly in regions like the Mekong Basin where secondary cities face difficulties accessing reliable and sustainable water sources. This brief highlights the importance of addressing water loss reduction through integrated agroforestry and micro-hydro systems.

## Introduction to Water Scarcity in Secondary Cities

Secondary cities in the Mekong Basin are affected by climate change impacts on agriculture, including erratic rainfall patterns. This has led to reduced crop yields and economic instability for local communities. Resilient irrigation methods are essential for addressing these challenges.
