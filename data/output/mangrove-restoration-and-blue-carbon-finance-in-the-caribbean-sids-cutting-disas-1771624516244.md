### Analysis

The water security crisis in Sub-Saharan Africa necessitates immediate attention due to its severe impacts on public health, economic productivity, and social stability. Access to clean drinking water is essential for preventing waterborne diseases such as cholera, which claimed nearly 300,000 lives globally between 2014-2018 (WHO/UNICEF Joint Monitoring Programme). Additionally, businesses struggle to operate effectively without stable power sources, hindering job creation and economic growth.
