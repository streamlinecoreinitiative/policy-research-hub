The Sahel region faces severe water scarcity, food insecurity, and economic instability due to climate change. ## Section 4: Policy Recommendations and Future Research Directions

To support the adoption of agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems, policy recommendations include:

1. **Financial Incentives**: Governments can provide grants or low-interest loans to support project development.
2. **Regulatory Frameworks**: Policymakers should establish regulations that encourage private sector investment in renewable energy and water conservation technologies.
3. **Capacity Building**: Training programs for farmers, technicians, and policymakers are essential for successful implementation.

Future research directions include:

1. **Economic Impact Analysis**: Assessing the long-term economic benefits of agroforestry-micro-hydro systems.
