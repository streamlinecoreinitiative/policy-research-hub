### Executive Summary

To address the pressing challenges of water security, clean energy access, and climate adaptation in Sub-Saharan Africa, we recommend that governments prioritize investments in safe drinking water infrastructure, renewable energy projects, and climate-resilient practices. Specifically, we urge policymakers to:

* Increase investment in water infrastructure projects such as wells and pipelines to improve access to safe drinking water.

By adopting these measures, governments can significantly improve living standards while contributing to broader sustainable development goals.
