# Flood-Resilient Transport Corridors in the Sudano-Sahelian Belt: Lowering Emissions from Critical Sectors

## Executive Summary

The Sudano-Sahelian belt is highly vulnerable to climate change, experiencing frequent and severe flooding that disrupts transportation networks, affects livelihoods, and exacerbates poverty. This brief highlights the importance of flood-resilient transport corridors in this region, emphasizing the need for integrated approaches combining water security, clean energy, and climate adaptation strategies.
