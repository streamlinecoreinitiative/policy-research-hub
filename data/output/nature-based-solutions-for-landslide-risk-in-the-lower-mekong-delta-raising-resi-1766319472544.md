### Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Lower Mekong Delta

The Lower Mekong delta is a critical region for food security and economic stability in Southeast Asia. Climate change has significantly impacted the region's water resources, leading to severe droughts and floods.

### Current Irrigation Practices and Challenges

Existing irrigation systems in the Lower Mekong delta are often inefficient and prone to water loss through evaporation and seepage. The reliance on diesel generators for energy contributes to greenhouse gas emissions and air pollution.
