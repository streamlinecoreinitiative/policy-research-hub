## Section 2: Mangrove Restoration and Blue Carbon Finance

Mangroves are critical ecosystems that provide a range of ecosystem services, including:

* Shoreline protection against storms and erosion
* Habitat provision for fisheries and biodiversity
* Water filtration and sedimentation reduction
* Carbon sequestration through blue carbon finance

Blue carbon finance refers to the monetization of carbon credits generated from coastal ecosystems like mangroves. This approach can provide a cost-effective solution for enhancing water security, promoting sustainable livelihoods, and mitigating the impacts of droughts.

### Key Benefits:

* Mangrove restoration can enhance shoreline resilience against storms and erosion
* Blue carbon finance can generate revenue streams for local communities while sequestering carbon
