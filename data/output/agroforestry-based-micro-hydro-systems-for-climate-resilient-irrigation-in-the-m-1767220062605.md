### Executive Summary

The Mesoamerican Highlands face severe water scarcity challenges exacerbated by climate change, affecting agricultural productivity and food security. This brief explores an integrated solution combining agroforestry practices with micro-hydro systems to enhance irrigation efficiency and resilience.

### Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Mesoamerican Highlands

Climate change has significantly impacted the Mesoamerican Highlands, leading to droughts, floods, and unpredictable weather patterns. Agriculture is particularly vulnerable to these changes, with crop yields declining due to water scarcity. Resilient irrigation methods are essential for ensuring food security and economic stability.

#### Agroforestry’s Role in Climate Resilience

Agroforestry practices improve soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration. Integrating trees into agricultural landscapes:

• Enhances water retention, reducing erosion and runoff
• Increases soil fertility, improving crop yields
• Conserves biodiversity, supporting ecosystem services

#### Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency

Micro-hydro technology harnesses the energy of small water flows to generate electricity, offering benefits such as reduced reliance on diesel generators and increased energy efficiency.

### Integration of Agroforestry and Micro-Hydro in Climate-Resilient Water Supply

Pilot projects have demonstrated the effectiveness of combining agroforestry with micro-hydro systems:

• Improved water retention and reduced erosion
• Enhanced crop yields and increased income for farmers
• Reduced energy consumption and lower costs

### Policy Recommendations and Future Research Directions

To support widespread adoption, policy recommendations include:

1. **Financial incentives**: Provide subsidies or low-interest loans to encourage adoption of agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems.
2. **Capacity building**: Offer training programs for farmers and technicians on installation, maintenance, and operation of micro-hydro systems.
3. **Regulatory frameworks**: Establish policies supporting the integration of renewable energy sources into irrigation systems.
