**The Role of Agroforestry Systems**
Agroforestry systems involve integrating trees into agricultural landscapes to promote ecological interactions between trees and crops. These systems contribute to dual water security by:

• Rainwater harvesting: Trees help to intercept and store rainwater, reducing runoff and increasing soil moisture.
• Soil conservation: Tree roots hold soil in place, preventing erosion and landslides.

**Biochar's Potential in Soil Management**
Biochar is a sustainable soil amendment made from organic materials that have been heated to high temperatures. Biochar improves soil structure, increases organic matter, and enhances water retention. These benefits can lead to increased agricultural productivity, improved crop yields, and enhanced ecosystem services.
