**Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Andean Highlands**

### Executive Summary

The Andean Highlands are facing severe water scarcity due to climate change, impacting agriculture and food security. This project proposal presents an innovative solution: integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems for climate-resilient irrigation. By combining these two approaches, we can address water scarcity while promoting sustainable agriculture and energy efficiency.

### Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Andean Highlands

Climate change is altering rainfall patterns in the Andean Highlands, leading to severe water scarcity for agricultural purposes. This has significant implications for food security and economic stability in the region. Irrigation systems currently in use are often inefficient, relying on boreholes or canals that lose a substantial amount of water through evaporation and seepage.

### Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Water Supply

Agroforestry is a land-use practice that integrates trees into agricultural landscapes. It offers numerous benefits, including improved soil health, biodiversity conservation, and carbon sequestration. By combining agroforestry with micro-hydro systems, we can create a climate-resilient water supply for irrigation.

**Benefits of Agroforestry:**

• Improved soil health through tree root growth
• Enhanced biodiversity through habitat creation
• Carbon sequestration through tree growth

**Micro-Hydro Systems:**

• Small-scale hydroelectric systems that harness energy from rivers and streams
• Reduced reliance on diesel generators and increased energy efficiency

### Integration of Agroforestry and Micro-Hydro in Climate-Resilient Water Supply

Pilot projects have demonstrated the effectiveness of integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems for climate-resilient irrigation. These integrated systems not only provide a reliable water supply but also promote sustainable agriculture and energy production.
