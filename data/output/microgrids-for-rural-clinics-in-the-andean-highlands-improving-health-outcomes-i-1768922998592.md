	* Invest $500 million annually for solar photovoltaic installations across Sub-Saharan Africa (UNFCCC, 2024).
	* Implement feed-in tariffs or other financial incentives to encourage renewable energy adoption.
2. **Enhance Water Security**:
	* Allocate [VERIFY] $3 billion over five years for climate-smart irrigation projects in vulnerable regions (World Bank, 2025).
	* Promote water-efficient crops and practices among smallholder farmers.
3. **Promote Clean Cooking Solutions**:
	* Provide subsidies for clean cookstoves to reduce indoor air pollution (WHO, 2024).
	* Collaborate with NGOs and local governments to distribute stoves in rural areas where access is limited.
