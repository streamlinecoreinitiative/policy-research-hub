# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Andean Highlands: A Sustainable Solution for Water-Stressed Communities

## Executive Summary

Climate change is exacerbating water scarcity in the Andean highlands, posing significant threats to food security and economic stability. This project proposal outlines an innovative approach to address this challenge through the integration of agroforestry and micro-hydro systems for climate-resilient irrigation.
