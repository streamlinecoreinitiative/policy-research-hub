## Executive Summary

## Policy Recommendations

1. **Expand Renewable Energy Projects**:
   - Implement solar-powered desalination plants in coastal regions with limited freshwater resources.
   - Deploy small-scale wind turbines and solar panels in off-grid areas, supported by government subsidies (World Bank, 2023).
2. **Strengthen Water Infrastructure**:
   - Invest in rainwater harvesting systems to augment existing water sources, especially in arid zones where rainfall is unpredictable.
   - Upgrade boreholes and wells with modern filtration technologies to ensure safe drinking water access (UN-Water/WHO, 2022).
3. **Enhance Early Warning Systems**:
   - Establish comprehensive early warning systems for droughts and floods using satellite imagery and ground sensors.
   - Train local communities in disaster preparedness and response to enhance resilience.
