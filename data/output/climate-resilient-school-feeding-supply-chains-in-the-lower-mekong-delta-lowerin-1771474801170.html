Policy Recommendations

To address these critical issues, we recommend the following:

1. Increase Access to Safe Drinking Water: Invest in water infrastructure projects such as wells, boreholes, and rainwater harvesting systems. Prioritize rural areas where access is most lacking. Implement community-based management models for sustainable long-term use.

  1. Accelerate Renewable Energy Deployment: Encourage private sector investment through tax incentives, grants, and regulatory frameworks that support the growth of renewable energy projects. Focus on off-grid solar solutions to reach remote communities not served by grid electricity.
  2. Enhance Climate Adaptation Measures: Develop early warning systems for climate-related disasters using satellite data and ground-based sensors. Implement drought-resistant crops and water-efficient irrigation technologies in agriculture. Provide micro-insurance schemes that cover smallholder farmers against weather-induced losses, ensuring financial stability during crises.
