## Policy Recommendations

To enhance water security and promote clean energy use, we recommend the following policy interventions:

1. **Increase Access to Safe Drinking Water:**
	* Invest in improved infrastructure like piped systems, boreholes, and rainwater harvesting ponds.
	* Implement community-based management schemes for sustainable maintenance.
	* Source: World Bank Open Data (2024)
2. **Promote Clean Energy Solutions:**
	* Encourage the deployment of solar-powered water pumping systems to reduce energy costs and improve access to clean water.
	* Support research and development in decentralized wind turbines for off-grid areas.
	* Source: World Bank Open Data (2023)
3. **Enhance Climate Adaptation Measures:**
	* Develop early warning systems for droughts and floods, along with improved agricultural practices that are climate-resilient.
	* Implement community-based disaster preparedness plans to mitigate the impact of extreme weather events.
	* Sources: NOAA climate data, UN-Water/WHO (2022)
