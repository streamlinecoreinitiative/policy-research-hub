# Water Loss Reduction in Secondary Cities in the Horn of Africa: Cutting Disaster Losses for Vulnerable Groups

## Executive Summary

The Horn of Africa is one of the most water-scarce regions globally, exacerbating poverty and food insecurity among its populations. This brief focuses on reducing water loss in secondary cities through integrated solutions combining agroforestry with micro-hydro systems to enhance climate resilience.

## Water Scarcity and Climate Change Impacts

The Horn of Africa is characterized by severe water scarcity, exacerbated by climate change impacts such as rising temperatures and altered rainfall patterns. These changes have significant implications for agriculture, the primary source of livelihoods in most countries.
