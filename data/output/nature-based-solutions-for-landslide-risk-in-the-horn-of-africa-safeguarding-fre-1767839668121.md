# Nature-Based Solutions for Landslide Risk in the Horn of Africa: Safeguarding Freshwater Ecosystems

## Executive Summary

The Horn of Africa region is prone to severe water scarcity and climate-related disasters, including landslides that threaten freshwater ecosystems. This brief presents a nature-based solution to address these challenges through integrated agroforestry and micro-hydro systems.
