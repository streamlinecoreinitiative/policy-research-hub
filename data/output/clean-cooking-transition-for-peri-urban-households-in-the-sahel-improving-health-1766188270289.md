The Ethiopian Highlands are a critical agricultural region facing severe water scarcity due to climate change. Erratic rainfall patterns and increased evaporation from highland wetlands have significant impacts on agriculture. Traditional irrigation methods exacerbate poverty among smallholder farmers by being energy-intensive and costly. The World Bank reports that renewable energy adoption is crucial for Africa's economic growth, with a focus on solar and wind power (World Bank, 2020).
