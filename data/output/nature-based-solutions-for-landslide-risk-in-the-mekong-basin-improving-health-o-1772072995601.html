Bridging Water Security, Clean Energy, and Climate Resilience in Sub-Saharan Africa

Sub-Saharan Africa is facing a triple challenge of water scarcity, energy poverty, and climate vulnerability. To address these interconnected issues, we recommend implementing cost-effective, scalable solutions for both water security and renewable energy. This policy brief provides actionable recommendations tailored specifically for government officials, NGO leaders, and development practitioners.

    Sub-Saharan Africa is characterized by limited access to safe drinking water (32.4%) and electricity (53.3%), exacerbating existing challenges.
