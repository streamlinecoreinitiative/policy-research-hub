Climate Resilient Water Security and Renewable Energy in Sub-Saharan Africa: Strengthening Community Livelihoods

Executive Summary

Sub-Saharan Africa faces significant challenges in ensuring access to safe drinking water, reliable electricity, and renewable energy sources. This region is particularly vulnerable to climate change impacts such as erratic rainfall patterns and rising temperatures, exacerbating existing water scarcity issues and limiting the adoption of clean energy solutions. To address these pressing needs, we recommend implementing community-based water supply systems, promoting renewable energy integration into rural power grids, and strengthening climate adaptation strategies.
