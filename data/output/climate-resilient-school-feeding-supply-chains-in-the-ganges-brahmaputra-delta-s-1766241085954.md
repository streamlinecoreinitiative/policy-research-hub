Micro-hydro technology offers a promising solution for efficient irrigation. Benefits include:

*   **Reduced reliance on diesel generators:** Micro-hydro systems eliminate the need for fossil fuels, reducing greenhouse gas emissions and dependence on imported energy.
*   **Increased energy efficiency:** Micro-hydro systems can achieve higher energy conversion rates than traditional power generation methods.
