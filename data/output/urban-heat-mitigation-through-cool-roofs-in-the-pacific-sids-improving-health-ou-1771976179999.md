## Policy Recommendations

To address this challenge, we recommend the following:

1. **Develop and Implement Cool Roof Policies:**
	* Governments should establish policies promoting cool roof technologies, such as reflective coatings or green roofs.
	* Incentives can be provided for building owners to adopt cool roof technologies, including tax breaks or subsidies.
2. **Increase Access to Electricity for Cooling:**
	* Invest in renewable energy sources, like solar power, to increase access to electricity for cooling.
	* Develop and implement programs to provide affordable cooling solutions for low-income households.
3. **Enhance Building Codes and Inspections:**
	* Update building codes to include requirements for cool roofs and proper insulation.
	* Regular inspections can ensure compliance with new regulations.

- **Cost:** Initial investments in cool roof technologies may be substantial, but long-term benefits include reduced heat-related illnesses and mortality rates. Funding can come from international climate finance, national budgets, and private sector partnerships.
