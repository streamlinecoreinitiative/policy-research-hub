## Context & Challenge

Sub-Saharan Africa faces critical water security and energy access issues, exacerbated by climate change. The region has the lowest access to safely managed drinking water (32.4%) compared to other regions globally (73.7%), according to World Bank data [1]. Limited electricity access (53.3%) also hinders economic development and environmental sustainability [2]. Climate change intensifies these challenges through recurrent droughts and floods, making it imperative to bolster resilience strategies.
