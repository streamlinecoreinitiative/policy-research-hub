	* Invest in infrastructure projects to improve access to safe drinking water, such as constructing and rehabilitating wells and boreholes.
	* Implement cost-sharing models with communities to reduce initial costs and ensure sustainability.
2. **Clean Energy**:
	* Encourage the use of solar-powered mini-grids for remote areas where grid extension is not feasible.
	* Develop policies that incentivize renewable energy adoption, such as feed-in tariffs or tax rebates for small-scale renewable projects.
3. **Climate Adaptation**:
	* Mobilize climate finance to support adaptation measures like early warning systems and water management infrastructure in vulnerable regions.
	* Promote the use of drought-resistant crops and improve irrigation systems to enhance agricultural resilience during dry spells.
