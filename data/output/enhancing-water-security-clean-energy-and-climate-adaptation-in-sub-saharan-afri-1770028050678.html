This policy brief recommends investing in cost-effective solutions focused on water security, clean energy, and climate adaptation to improve living standards and resilience.

Policy Recommendations

1. Invest in water supply infrastructure projects: Implement cost-effective solutions such as boreholes and rainwater harvesting systems that are locally sustainable.

  • Promote community-based initiatives for safe water management: Support hygiene education and water treatment technologies through local partnerships.
  • Encourage the adoption of solar-powered mini-grids: Provide electricity access where grid expansion is not feasible, prioritizing vulnerable populations.
  • Implement policies promoting renewable energy investments: Leverage grants, tax incentives, and feed-in tariffs to support renewable energy growth.
  • Support indigenous knowledge systems in climate adaptation planning: Collaborate with local communities for sustainable land use practices and early warning systems.
