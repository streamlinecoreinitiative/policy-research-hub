## Executive Summary

Sub-Saharan Africa faces significant challenges in water security, renewable energy access, and climate resilience. With only 32.4% of the population having access to safely managed drinking water (World Bank Open Data, 2024), there is a critical need for innovative solutions that can accelerate progress towards SDG 6 targets. This policy brief outlines actionable steps for enhancing water security, renewable energy access, and climate resilience through evidence-based interventions.

To address these challenges, we recommend:

1. **Enhanced Water Security**: Implement community-led water harvesting systems and improve access to clean drinking water through infrastructure projects.
2. **Accelerated Renewable Energy Access**: Promote solar-powered renewable energy initiatives, particularly in remote areas with high potential for solar power generation.
3. **Climate Resilience Measures**: Invest in climate-smart agricultural practices to enhance resilience against extreme weather events.

This warming not only threatens water security but also exacerbates existing vulnerabilities like droughts and heatwaves.

## Policy Recommendations

To address these critical challenges, we recommend the following policy interventions:

1. **Enhanced Water Security**: Implement community-led water harvesting systems and improve access to clean drinking water through infrastructure projects.
	* Allocate more funding towards rural piped water schemes and ensure equitable distribution of resources.
2. **Accelerated Renewable Energy Access**: Promote solar-powered renewable energy initiatives, particularly in remote areas with high potential for solar power generation.
	* Encourage public-private partnerships for financing large-scale renewable energy projects like hydroelectric dams and wind farms.
3. **Climate Resilience Measures**: Invest in climate-smart agricultural practices to enhance resilience against extreme weather events such as droughts and floods.
	* Develop early warning systems and implement disaster preparedness plans, including targeted financial support for affected communities.
