## Policy Recommendations

1. **Enhance Water Security**:
   - Implement water resource management strategies that include dam construction, desalination plants, and rainwater harvesting (World Bank, 2024).
   - Provide subsidies for household water filters in low-income areas.
   - Support community-based water management initiatives to ensure equitable access.

2. **Increase Clean Energy Access**:
   - Invest in solar-powered mini-grids and micro-hydro systems for rural communities (World Bank, 2023).
   - Establish off-grid renewable energy projects that leverage private sector investment.
   - Implement feed-in tariffs or community-based power cooperatives to reduce costs.

3. **Strengthen Climate Adaptation**:
   - Develop early-warning systems and climate-resilient infrastructure like flood barriers and irrigation canals (NOAA climate data).
   - Promote agricultural diversification and climate-smart agriculture practices.
   - Encourage insurance schemes for small-scale farmers to mitigate financial risks.

## Implementation Considerations

- **Costs**: Initial investments will require significant public funding, but long-term benefits in terms of health improvements and economic growth justify the cost (World Bank, 2024).
- **Barriers**: Political resistance, lack of infrastructure, and insufficient technical expertise can hinder implementation. Addressing these issues through capacity building programs and international partnerships is crucial.
- **Sustainability**: Ensure that interventions are sustainable by integrating them into national development plans and ensuring continuous monitoring and evaluation.
