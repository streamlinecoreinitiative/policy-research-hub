Policy Recommendations

To bridge these gaps and enhance resilience in Sub-Saharan Africa, we propose the following policy recommendations:

1. Solar Energy Integration: Invest in solar-powered water pumping systems and energy-efficient appliances for households and small businesses. This can reduce reliance on non-renewable sources while improving access to clean energy (World Bank Open Data, 2024).

  • Renewable Energy Finance: Establish a dedicated fund or partnership with international development banks to finance renewable energy projects in rural areas.   • Climate-Resilient Water Management: Promote watershed co-management initiatives involving indigenous communities and local governments. These partnerships can enhance water storage capacity through natural infrastructure like mangroves or small-scale reservoirs, thereby improving drought resilience (NOAA Climate).
