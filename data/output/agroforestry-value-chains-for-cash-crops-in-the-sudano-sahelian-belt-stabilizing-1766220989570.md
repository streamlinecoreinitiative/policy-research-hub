# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Sudano-Sahelian Belt

## Executive Summary

The Sudano-Sahelian belt faces severe water scarcity, exacerbating food insecurity and economic instability. This brief explores the potential of integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems to address this issue. Agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems can improve irrigation efficiency, reduce reliance on diesel generators, and increase energy efficiency.

## Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Sudano-Sahelian Belt

The Sudano-Sahelian belt is characterized by erratic rainfall patterns, resulting in severe water scarcity. Climate change has further exacerbated this issue, with average annual rainfall decreasing significantly over the past decade [verify]. This has led to a decline in agricultural productivity, food insecurity, and economic instability.
