**Executive Summary:**
The Mesoamerican Highlands face severe water scarcity, exacerbated by climate change impacts on agriculture. This brief highlights the potential of agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems to address these challenges through integrated solutions.

Existing irrigation systems in the Mesoamerican Highlands include boreholes, canals, and gravity-fed systems. These systems often suffer from high water loss rates due to evaporation and seepage.

**Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency**

Micro-hydro technology offers a reliable source of energy for irrigation systems. By harnessing small-scale hydroelectric power, farmers can reduce their reliance on diesel generators and decrease energy costs.
