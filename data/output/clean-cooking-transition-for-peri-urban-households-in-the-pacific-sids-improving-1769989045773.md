## Policy Recommendations

To enhance energy security and improve water management in Sub-Saharan Africa, the following recommendations are proposed:

1. **Invest in Renewable Energy Integration**: Develop microgrids or hybrid systems that incorporate solar, wind, and hydro power to provide reliable electricity for water supply infrastructure.
    - **Specific Steps**: Collaborate with local communities and NGOs to identify suitable renewable energy sources; prioritize projects based on cost-effectiveness and climate resilience.

2. **Implement Climate-Resilient Water Supply Systems**: Design and implement systems that are resilient to climate change, including drought-resistant technologies such as desalination plants or groundwater extraction.
    - **Specific Steps**: Conduct vulnerability assessments to determine the most suitable water supply solutions; engage local stakeholders in decision-making processes for project design and implementation.

3. **Strengthen Energy Access through Off-grid Solutions**: Promote off-grid solar-powered well systems, rainwater harvesting ponds, and energy-efficient pumps to improve access to clean water.
    - **Specific Steps**: Conduct feasibility studies and cost-benefit analyses to identify the most suitable off-grid solutions; provide technical support and training for community-based maintenance.
