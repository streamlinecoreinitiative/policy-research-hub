**Executive Summary**

The Andean highlands face severe water scarcity due to climate change, affecting agriculture and food security. This brief highlights the potential of agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems as a solution for climate-resilient irrigation. By integrating agroforestry practices with small-scale hydroelectric systems, communities can improve water retention, reduce erosion, and increase energy efficiency.

**Water Scarcity in the Andean Highlands**

Climate change impacts agriculture in the Andean highlands through erratic rainfall patterns, affecting crop yields and food security. Resilient irrigation methods are crucial for economic stability and community well-being. Current irrigation systems suffer from water loss through evaporation and seepage.
