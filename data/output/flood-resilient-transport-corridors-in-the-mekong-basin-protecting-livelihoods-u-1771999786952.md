## Policy Recommendations

To address these challenges, we recommend:

1. **Integrate Renewable Energy in Infrastructure**: Develop micro-grids powered by solar energy for off-grid areas to enhance electricity access and reduce reliance on non-renewable sources.
2. **Water Security Initiatives**: Implement rainwater harvesting systems, improve sanitation facilities, and invest in climate-resilient water supply systems such as drought-resistant crops and improved irrigation techniques.
3. **Climate Adaptation Programs**: Promote early warning systems for extreme weather events, support the construction of flood defenses, and encourage the adoption of agroforestry practices that enhance soil health and biodiversity.

## Implementation Considerations

To ensure the feasibility and financial sustainability of these recommendations:

1. **Conduct Cost-Benefit Analysis**: Determine the economic viability of renewable energy projects through thorough cost-benefit analyses.
2. **Establish Partnership Frameworks**: Foster partnerships between governments, NGOs, and private sector entities to align resources with project goals.
3. **Capacity Building**: Train local communities in maintenance and operation of solar-powered systems to foster self-sufficiency.
