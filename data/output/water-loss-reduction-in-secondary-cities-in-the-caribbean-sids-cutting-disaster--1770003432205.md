## Policy Recommendations

1. **Accelerate Renewable Energy Projects**: Invest in solar-powered water pumps, district heating systems for clinics, and microgrids to enhance energy access.
2. **Promote Clean Cooking Solutions**: Implement subsidies for clean cooking stoves to reduce indoor air pollution and improve health outcomes (World Bank, 2024).
3. **Climate-Smart Irrigation Cooperatives**: Develop cooperatives that use climate-resilient irrigation techniques like drip systems or rainwater harvesting.
4. **Support Micro-insurance Programs**: Offer micro-insurance for smallholder farmers to protect against drought-induced losses.
