# agroforestry value chains for cash crops in the Great Lakes region: supporting adaptation targets in NDCs

**Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Ethiopian Highlands**

**Executive Summary**

Climate change exacerbates water scarcity in the Ethiopian Highlands, impacting agriculture and smallholder farmers' livelihoods. This brief proposes an integrated approach combining agroforestry with micro-hydro systems for climate-resilient irrigation. The proposed solution improves soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration while providing sustainable energy for irrigation.
