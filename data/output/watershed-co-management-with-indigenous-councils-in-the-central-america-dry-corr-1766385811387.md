# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Ethiopian Highlands

## Executive Summary

Climate change impacts agriculture worldwide, characterized by erratic rainfall patterns and increased water scarcity. In the Ethiopian Highlands, traditional irrigation methods are energy-intensive and costly, leading to significant economic burdens on smallholder farmers. This brief proposes integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems as a solution to address severe water scarcity while promoting sustainable development goals in agriculture and renewable energy adoption.
