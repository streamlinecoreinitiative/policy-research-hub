Sub-Saharan Africa faces critical challenges in water security, clean energy access, and climate adaptation. ## Context & Challenge

Sub-Saharan Africa is home to nearly half a billion people lacking safe water resources and millions without reliable electricity (UN-Water/WHO). These numbers are even more concerning given that Sub-Saharan Africa's renewable energy consumption stands at only 19.7%, despite its vast potential in solar and wind power (World Bank Open Data).
