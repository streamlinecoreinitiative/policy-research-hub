# Mangrove Restoration and Blue Carbon Finance in the Pacific SIDS: Supporting Adaptation Targets in NDCs

## Executive Summary

Climate change poses significant threats to the livelihoods of Small Island Developing States (SIDS) in the Pacific, particularly regarding water security, clean energy, and climate adaptation. This brief highlights the importance of mangrove restoration and blue carbon finance as a means of supporting adaptation targets outlined in Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs). By integrating these approaches, SIDS can enhance their resilience to climate change impacts while promoting sustainable development.

## Section 2: Mangrove Restoration as a Climate Adaptation Strategy

Mangroves play a critical role in supporting the livelihoods of Pacific Islanders, providing:

*   **Shoreline protection**: Mangroves act as natural barriers against storms and sea level rise
*   **Fisheries and aquaculture**: Mangroves support diverse marine life, including fish, shellfish, and other species

However, mangrove forests are under threat due to:

*   **Deforestation and habitat destruction**: Coastal development, agriculture, and logging contribute to mangrove loss
*   **Climate-related stressors**: Rising sea levels, increased storm frequency, and warmer temperatures further threaten mangrove health

## Section 3: Blue Carbon Finance as a Means of Supporting Mangrove Restoration

Blue carbon finance refers to the financial mechanisms used to support the conservation and restoration of coastal ecosystems, including mangroves. This approach can:

*   **Mobilize funding**: Blue carbon finance provides a means of accessing financing for mangrove restoration and conservation efforts
*   **Promote sustainable development**: By supporting eco-friendly projects, blue carbon finance contributes to broader sustainable development goals

## Section 4: Supporting Adaptation Targets in NDCs through Mangrove Restoration and Blue Carbon Finance

Pacific SIDS can support their adaptation targets outlined in NDCs by:

*   **Integrating mangrove restoration into national climate plans**: Incorporating mangrove conservation and restoration into national climate policies
*   **Mobilizing blue carbon finance**: Accessing financing for mangrove restoration and conservation efforts through blue carbon finance mechanisms
