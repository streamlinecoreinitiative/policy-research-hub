2. **Promote Renewable Energy Adoption**: Encourage the use of renewable energy sources such as solar power to reduce reliance on fossil fuels, thereby improving both energy security and environmental sustainability.
3. **Enhance Climate Resilience Programs**: Develop comprehensive climate adaptation programs that integrate early warning systems and disaster preparedness measures.
