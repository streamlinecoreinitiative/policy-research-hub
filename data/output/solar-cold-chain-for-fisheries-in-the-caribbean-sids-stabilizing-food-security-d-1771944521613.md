## Analysis

Our analysis highlights the critical role of water security and clean energy access in fostering sustainable development. In the Caribbean SIDS, limited access to safe drinking water exposes populations to health risks, exacerbating existing vulnerabilities. Moreover, the region's underinvestment in renewable energy perpetuates reliance on fossil fuels, contributing to rising greenhouse gas emissions and intensifying climate impacts.
