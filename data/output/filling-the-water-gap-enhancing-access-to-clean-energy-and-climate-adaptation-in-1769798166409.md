Sub-Saharan Africa faces significant water scarcity and energy poverty crises, with only 32.4% of its population having access to safely managed drinking water (World Bank Open Data, 2024). 2. **Increase Renewable Energy Use**: Implement policies promoting solar energy adoption and improve grid infrastructure for electricity supply (World Bank Open Data, 2023).
3. **Promote Climate-Friendly Practices**: Encourage community-based climate adaptation initiatives through grants and training programs.
