### Executive Summary

The Sahel region is highly vulnerable to climate change, with severe water scarcity affecting agriculture and livelihoods. This brief explores the potential of mangrove restoration and blue carbon finance as a resilience-building strategy for communities in the Sahel.

Traditional irrigation methods in the Sahel region rely on borehole pumps and canal systems. These systems consume significant amounts of energy and result in substantial water losses.

Mangroves play a critical role in maintaining coastal ecosystems and providing resilience to climate change impacts. Benefits of mangrove restoration include:

### Section 4: Blue Carbon Finance for Sustainable Development

Blue carbon finance mechanisms can provide a critical source of funding for mangrove restoration and conservation efforts. Benefits include:

* Reduced greenhouse gas emissions
* Enhanced ecosystem services (e.g., coastal protection, fisheries)
* Improved livelihoods for local communities

Pilot projects in the Sahel region have demonstrated the feasibility of blue carbon finance mechanisms.
