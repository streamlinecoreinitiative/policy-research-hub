## Policy Recommendations

### 1. Solar-Powered Wells in Rural Areas
Install solar-powered well pumps to improve access to safe drinking water in rural communities (World Bank, 2024). This technology reduces reliance on grid electricity and ensures continuous supply during power outages.

### 2. Renewable Energy Microgrids for Rural Clinics
Deploy microgrid systems powered by renewable energy sources to provide reliable electricity to remote healthcare facilities, reducing health risks associated with unreliable power supplies (World Bank, 2023).

### 3. Climate-Resilient Irrigation Systems
Implement climate-smart irrigation schemes that incorporate drought-resistant crops and water-efficient technologies in arid regions (UN SDG Report, 2023). This approach ensures sustainable water use and reduces vulnerability to changing rainfall patterns.
