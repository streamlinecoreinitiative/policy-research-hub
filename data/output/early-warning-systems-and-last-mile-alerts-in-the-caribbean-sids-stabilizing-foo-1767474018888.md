## Executive Summary

The Caribbean Small Island Developing States (SIDS) are highly vulnerable to droughts, which can have devastating impacts on food security and economic stability. This brief proposes a comprehensive approach to address water scarcity through integrated agroforestry and micro-hydro systems in the Andean Highlands.
