# Solar Cold-Chain for Fisheries in the Mekong Basin: Improving Health Outcomes in Heatwaves

## Executive Summary

Climate change poses significant challenges to fisheries in the Mekong Basin, particularly during heatwaves when water temperatures rise and fish populations decline. In this context, implementing solar-powered cold-chain systems can improve health outcomes by reducing post-harvest losses and ensuring a steady supply of fresh fish. This brief highlights the benefits of microfinance-powered solar irrigation systems for small farmers in the Kenyan Highlands as a model for addressing similar challenges in the Mekong Basin.
