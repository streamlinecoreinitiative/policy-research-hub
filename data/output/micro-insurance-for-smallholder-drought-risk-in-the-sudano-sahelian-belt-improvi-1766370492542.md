# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Mesoamerican Highlands

## Executive Summary

The Mesoamerican Highlands face severe water scarcity challenges due to climate change, impacting agriculture and economic stability. This brief proposes an integrated solution combining agroforestry with micro-hydro systems to address these issues. Agroforestry enhances soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration, while micro-hydro technology increases irrigation efficiency and reduces reliance on diesel generators. Pilot projects demonstrate the combined use of agroforestry and micro-hydro, showcasing cost-effectiveness and potential for scaling up. This approach contributes to sustainable development goals in agriculture and renewable energy adoption.

### Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency

Micro-hydro technology is a small-scale hydroelectric system designed to harness energy from water flow. This approach reduces reliance on diesel generators and increases energy efficiency, making it an attractive solution for irrigation systems.
