### Executive Summary

The Central African Rainforest faces severe water scarcity due to climate change, including erratic rainfall patterns and increased evaporation from wetlands. Traditional irrigation methods are energy-intensive and costly, exacerbating economic challenges for smallholder farmers in vulnerable communities. This project proposal outlines a comprehensive plan to address these issues by integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems.
