## Policy Recommendations
To address these challenges, we propose the following policy recommendations:

1. **Expand Renewable Energy Projects**: Invest in solar and wind power projects to increase renewable energy consumption from current levels of 19.7% to at least 30%. This will not only improve access to clean energy but also create local jobs and stimulate economic growth.
