# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Ethiopian Highlands

## Executive Summary

The Ethiopian Highlands face severe water scarcity due to climate change-induced erratic rainfall patterns, impacting agriculture and food security. Traditional irrigation methods are energy-intensive and costly, while micro-hydro systems offer a sustainable and renewable energy solution. Integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems can improve soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration, making them a cost-effective and environmentally friendly option for climate-resilient water supply.
