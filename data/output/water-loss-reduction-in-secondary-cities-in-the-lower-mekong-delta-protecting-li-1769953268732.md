## Policy Recommendations

1. **Expand Public-Private Partnerships for Water Infrastructure**: Governments should collaborate with private sector entities to fund and develop water treatment facilities in underserved regions. By leveraging investment from international donors and local businesses, countries can rapidly increase access to clean drinking water.
2. **Promote Solar Power Adoption**: Encourage rural electrification through solar-powered solutions such as mini-grids and off-grid systems. Implement subsidies for solar panels and batteries to reduce initial costs and make them more accessible to low-income households.
3. **Enhance Climate Adaptation Strategies**: Develop early warning systems for extreme weather events like droughts and floods. These systems should include community-based monitoring of water sources, improved agricultural practices, and disaster preparedness training programs.
