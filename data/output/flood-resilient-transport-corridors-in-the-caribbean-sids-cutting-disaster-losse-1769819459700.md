To address these issues, we recommend investing in renewable energy projects for decentralized power generation, strengthening water supply systems with improved sanitation facilities, and implementing climate adaptation strategies tailored to local conditions.

## Policy Recommendations

1. **Invest in Renewable Energy Projects**
	* Develop microgrids for rural clinics to provide reliable power.
	* Implement solar-powered desalination plants to improve freshwater supply in arid regions.

2. **Strengthen Water Supply Systems**
	* Build rainwater harvesting ponds and ensure proper sanitation facilities.
	* Fund water treatment plants to reduce contamination of sources.

3. **Enhance Climate Adaptation Strategies**
	* Develop early warning systems for climate-related disasters.
	* Promote drought-resistant crops suitable for local climates.
