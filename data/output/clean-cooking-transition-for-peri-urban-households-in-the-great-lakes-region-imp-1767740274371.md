# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Ethiopian Highlands

## Executive Summary

The proposal focuses on addressing severe water scarcity through integrated agroforestry and micro-hydro systems in the Ethiopian Highlands. This approach combines traditional irrigation methods with modern, renewable energy solutions to provide climate-resilient water supply.

Climate change has significantly impacted the Ethiopian Highlands, leading to severe water scarcity. Average annual rainfall ranges from 800-1200 mm, with precipitation variability between 600-1400 mm annually.
