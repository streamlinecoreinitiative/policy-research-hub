The Horn of Africa is one of the most water-scarce regions globally, characterized by severe droughts and erratic rainfall patterns that affect agricultural productivity and food security. Climate-smart irrigation cooperatives can provide a sustainable solution by integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems.
