1. **Implement Water Sector Reforms**:
	* Invest in WWTP and improve sanitation facilities.
	* Promote rainwater harvesting systems, which can reduce reliance on groundwater.
2. **Expand Renewable Energy Projects**:
	* Increase investment in solar, wind, and hydroelectric power generation.
	* Develop community-based renewable energy cooperatives for decentralized electricity supply.
3. **Enhance Climate Resilience Measures**:
	* Strengthen early warning systems to predict climate-related disasters such as floods and droughts.
	* Promote disaster risk reduction programs that include building resilient infrastructure.
