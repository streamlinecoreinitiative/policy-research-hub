**Executive Summary**

The Ethiopian Highlands face severe water scarcity due to climate change impacts on rainfall patterns, affecting agriculture and livelihoods. This brief explores an integrated solution combining agroforestry and micro-hydro systems to address this challenge. Agroforestry improves soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration, while micro-hydro systems provide sustainable and renewable energy for irrigation.

Climate change affects rainfall patterns in the Ethiopian Highlands, leading to severe water scarcity. Traditional irrigation methods are energy-intensive and costly, consuming significant amounts of energy.
