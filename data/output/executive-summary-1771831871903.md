## Policy Recommendations

1. **Enhanced Access to Safe Drinking Water**:
	* Increase funding for water supply projects, particularly in rural areas.
	* Implement cost-sharing models between government and communities (World Bank, 2024).
	* Support small-scale water harvesting initiatives using community-led approaches (UN-Water/WHO, 2022).

2. **Promoting Renewable Energy Adoption**:
	* Invest in renewable energy infrastructure such as solar farms and mini-grids.
	* Provide subsidies for households and businesses to adopt renewable technologies (World Bank, [NEEDS SOURCE]).
	* Implement feed-in tariffs to incentivize private sector investment in clean energy projects.

3. **Climate-Resilient Infrastructure**:
	* Develop early warning systems for droughts and floods using satellite imagery and IoT sensors.
	* Build flood-resistant water supply networks and reservoirs (World Bank, 2024).
	* Strengthen institutional capacity to manage climate risks through training programs and knowledge sharing platforms.

## Implementation Considerations

- **Funding**: Mobilize additional resources from international donors and domestic budgets for sustainable development projects.
- **Partnerships**: Establish collaborative frameworks between governments, NGOs, and private sector entities for project implementation.
