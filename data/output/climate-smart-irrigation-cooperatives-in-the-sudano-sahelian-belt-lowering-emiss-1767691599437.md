**Climate-Smart Irrigation Cooperatives in the Sudano-Sahelian Belt: Lowering Emissions from Critical Sectors**

**Executive Summary**

The Sudano-Sahelian belt is highly vulnerable to climate change, facing severe water scarcity and food insecurity. This brief proposes an integrated approach to address these challenges through agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems for climate-resilient irrigation in the Ethiopian Highlands. By combining sustainable agriculture practices with renewable energy solutions, this initiative aims to reduce greenhouse gas emissions from critical sectors while improving livelihoods and promoting environmental resilience.
