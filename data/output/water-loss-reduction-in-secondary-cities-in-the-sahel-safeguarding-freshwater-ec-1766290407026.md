## Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency

Micro-hydro technology harnesses the power of small-scale hydroelectric systems to provide renewable energy for irrigation pumps. This approach reduces reliance on diesel generators, increasing energy efficiency and decreasing greenhouse gas emissions.
