### **Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Andean Highlands**

The Andean highlands are vulnerable to climate change impacts on agriculture, including erratic rainfall patterns. Resilient irrigation methods are crucial for food security and economic stability. Climate data indicates that the region has experienced changes in rainfall patterns over the past decade.
