## Agroforestry’s Role in Climate Resilience

Agroforestry has been recognized as a key strategy for enhancing climate resilience in agricultural landscapes. Its benefits include:

• **Soil health**: Agroforestry systems promote soil fertility, structure, and biodiversity.
• **Biodiversity conservation**: By integrating trees into farming practices, agroforestry helps maintain ecosystem services and promotes ecological balance.
• **Carbon sequestration**: Agroforestry can contribute significantly to carbon storage through the growth of woody vegetation.

## Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency

Micro-hydro technology offers a promising solution for efficient irrigation. Small-scale hydroelectric systems can:

• **Reduce reliance on diesel generators**: By harnessing the energy potential of water flows, micro-hydro systems decrease dependence on non-renewable energy sources.
• **Increase energy efficiency**: Micro-hydro systems typically have higher energy conversion rates compared to traditional power generation methods.
