## Policy Recommendations

1. **Invest in Renewable Energy Infrastructure**:
   - Develop solar-powered mini-grids and biogas systems to provide electricity where grid access is limited.
   - Encourage public-private partnerships (PPP) to finance these projects, leveraging existing renewable energy funds like the Global Environment Facility (GEF).
   
2. **Enhance Water Security**:
   - Implement community-managed water sources such as rainwater harvesting and boreholes in arid regions.
   - Promote water-efficient agricultural practices that reduce demand for irrigation water.
   - Strengthen institutional frameworks, including water management committees, to ensure sustainable use of scarce resources.

3. **Climate Resilience Measures**:
   - Invest in early warning systems for droughts and floods to mitigate the impact on vulnerable communities.
   - Promote afforestation and agroforestry projects that enhance biodiversity and improve soil health, contributing to climate resilience.

4. **Strengthen Governance and Financing Mechanisms**:
   - Establish clear regulatory frameworks that promote equitable access to water and electricity services.
   - Mobilize domestic resources through fiscal incentives and international aid for sustainable development projects.
