## Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Central America Dry Corridor

The Central America dry corridor is characterized by limited rainfall, high temperatures, and increased evaporation from vast wetlands. Climate change has further exacerbated these conditions, leading to severe water scarcity and related disasters. Smallholder farmers in vulnerable communities face significant economic challenges due to traditional irrigation methods that are energy-intensive and costly.
