## Policy Recommendations

1. **Invest in Climate-Resilient Infrastructure**:
	* Develop solar-powered irrigation systems to increase access to clean cooking fuels and reduce deforestation.
	* Implement early warning systems for droughts, floods, and other natural disasters using solar-powered technology.
2. **Promote Clean Cooking Initiatives**:
	* Deploy microgrids in rural clinics to provide reliable power, facilitating clean cooking solutions like biogas stoves.
3. **Integrate Climate Resilience into Water Security Programs**:
	* Engage indigenous councils for climate-resilient watershed management practices.
