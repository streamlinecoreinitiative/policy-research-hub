Policy Recommendations

1. Investment in Water Harvesting and Storage: Implement large-scale rainwater harvesting systems combined with small-scale household-level initiatives to increase access to clean drinking water (World Bank 2024).   1. Promote Renewable Energy Solutions: Encourage the deployment of solar-powered irrigation pumps and off-grid renewable energy systems in rural areas to enhance agricultural productivity and reduce dependence on imported fuels (World Bank 2023).
  2. Enhance Climate Resilience through Integrated Watershed Management: Collaborate with indigenous communities to implement watershed co-management models that integrate traditional knowledge systems with modern climate adaptation strategies (World Bank 2024).
