## Executive Summary

Immediate action is required to address the critical issues of water security, clean energy access, and climate resilience in Sub-Saharan Africa. ## Context & Challenge

Sub-Saharan Africa faces unprecedented challenges in accessing clean water, electricity, and adapting to climate change impacts. Recent data highlights the urgency of these issues:

* Only 32.4% of Sub-Saharan Africa's population has access to safely managed drinking water (World Bank, 2024) [Source: World Bank Open Data].
* Access to electricity is uneven, with only half of the population having reliable power (World Bank, 2023) [Source: World Bank Open Data].
* Climate change threatens agricultural productivity and water availability, exacerbating poverty, health risks, and economic disparities.

## Analysis

The analysis reveals significant gaps in water security, energy access, and climate adaptation:

* **Water Security Issues**: Sub-Saharan Africa suffers from inadequate sanitation facilities, leading to disease outbreaks (UN-Water/WHO, 2022) [Source: UN-Water/WHO].
* **Energy Access Gaps**: Renewable energy consumption is only 19.7% globally, with significant disparities in Sub-Saharan Africa.
* **Climate Vulnerability**: Climate change-induced weather patterns are causing food insecurity and displacement, affecting millions of people.

## Policy Recommendations

The following policy recommendations aim to address the critical issues outlined above:

1. **Expand Water Security**:
	* Invest [VERIFY] over 5 years to improve sanitation facilities and promote safe drinking water sources.
	* Collaborate with local communities to develop and implement sustainable water management practices.
2. **Promote Renewable Energy**:
	* Allocate [VERIFY] over 5 years for subsidies on solar panels and microgrids, especially in rural areas.
	* Train local communities in renewable energy maintenance and installation techniques.
3. **Enhance Climate Resilience**:
	* Invest [VERIFY] over 5 years to implement early warning systems and disaster preparedness plans that integrate climate change adaptation measures.
