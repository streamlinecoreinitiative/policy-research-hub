## Policy Recommendations

1. **Enhanced Water Security**:
	* Integrate WASH Programs: Implement water supply and sanitation projects in rural areas, focusing on community-led initiatives to ensure sustainable use.
	* Rainwater Harvesting and Wells: Invest in rainwater harvesting systems and solar-powered wells to improve access to safe drinking water.

2. **Promote Clean Energy Solutions**:
	* Renewable Energy Projects: Encourage investments in solar, wind, and hydroelectric power plants to reduce reliance on fossil fuels.
	* Energy Efficiency Measures: Implement energy-efficient appliances and promote the use of renewable heating systems (e.g., geothermal).

3. **Climate Adaptation Strategies**:
	* Early Warning Systems: Establish early warning systems for climate-related events like droughts, floods, and heatwaves to mitigate their impacts.
	* Afforestation Programs: Promote reforestation efforts to protect water sources and increase resilience against climate change.
