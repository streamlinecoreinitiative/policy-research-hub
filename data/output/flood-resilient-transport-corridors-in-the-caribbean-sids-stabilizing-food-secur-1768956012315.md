* The region lags behind the world average with only 53.3% of people having access to electricity (World Bank Open Data).
* Sub-Saharan Africa's renewable energy consumption (19.7%) falls far short of the SDG target of at least [VERIFY]% by 2030.

### Climate Adaptation

* Climate change impacts, including extreme weather events and resource scarcity, threaten agricultural productivity and public health systems in many Sub-Saharan African countries.
* Establishing early warning systems for droughts, floods, and other climate-related events can mitigate their impacts on food security and public health.

## Policy Recommendations

**Water Security:**

1. **Invest in Water Infrastructure**: Increase funding for water treatment plants, distribution networks, and sanitation facilities to improve access to safe drinking water.
2. **Promote Rainwater Harvesting**: Implement community-based rainwater harvesting programs to supplement existing water sources during dry periods.
3. **Educational Campaigns**: Launch public awareness campaigns to educate communities on proper hygiene practices and water conservation methods.

**Clean Energy Access:**

1. **Expand Renewable Energy Projects**: Invest in solar, wind, and hydropower projects to increase renewable energy consumption and reduce dependence on fossil fuels.
2. **Microgrids for Rural Areas**: Develop microgrid systems for off-grid areas to ensure reliable electricity supply at lower costs.
3. **Energy Efficiency Initiatives**: Support energy-efficient appliances and lighting solutions to reduce overall energy demand.

**Climate Adaptation:**

1. **Early Warning Systems**: Establish early warning systems for droughts, floods, and other climate-related events to mitigate their impacts on food security and public health.
2. **Watershed Management Programs**: Implement watershed management programs to protect water sources and enhance ecosystem resilience.
3. **Community-Based Climate Resilience Plans**: Encourage community-led initiatives in disaster preparedness and response strategies.
