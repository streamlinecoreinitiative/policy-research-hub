### Executive Summary

Sub-Saharan African countries face significant challenges in securing clean water, reliable electricity, and adapting to climate change impacts. To address these pressing issues, we recommend prioritizing investments in solar-powered wells, microgrids for rural clinics, early-warning systems, and nature-based solutions. These interventions can significantly improve access to clean drinking water, increase energy access, and enhance climate resilience.
