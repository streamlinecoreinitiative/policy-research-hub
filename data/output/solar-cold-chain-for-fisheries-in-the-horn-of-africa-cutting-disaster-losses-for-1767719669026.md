The Horn of Africa is one of the most vulnerable regions to climate change, facing severe water scarcity and poverty. ## Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Horn of Africa

The Horn of Africa is characterized by arid and semi-arid conditions, with limited rainfall and high evaporation rates. Climate change exacerbates these challenges, leading to droughts, floods, and increased temperature fluctuations.
