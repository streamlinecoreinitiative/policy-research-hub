In contrast, electricity access stands at 53.3%, significantly lower than global standards (91.6%) and highlighting regional disparities.

## Policy Recommendations

To address these challenges, governments should:

* Invest in solar-powered desalination plants to treat seawater for drinking water.
* Implement micro-insurance schemes targeting smallholder farmers affected by droughts.
* Develop community-based renewable energy cooperatives for decentralized power generation.
* Enhance early-warning systems for climate-related disasters using satellite imagery and IoT sensors.
