Rising temperatures, changing precipitation patterns, and increased evaporation from highland wetlands have exacerbated water scarcity. Traditional irrigation methods exacerbate poverty among smallholder farmers by being energy-intensive and costly.

	+ Rising temperatures
* Importance of resilient irrigation methods for food security:
