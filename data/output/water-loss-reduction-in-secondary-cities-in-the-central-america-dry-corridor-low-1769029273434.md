### Policy Recommendations

To address the challenges in Sub-Saharan Africa, we recommend the following:

1. **Expand Access to Clean Water**:
	* Implement community-led total sanitation (CLTS) programs to promote safe drinking water practices.
	* Invest in small-scale water treatment systems and distribution networks.
	* Increase funding for water resource management projects, particularly in rural areas where access is most limited.
2. **Promote Renewable Energy Access**:
	* Develop mini-grids and microgrids for off-grid communities.
	* Implement subsidies or grants to reduce the upfront cost of renewable energy technologies like solar panels and biogas digesters.
	* Encourage public-private partnerships (PPPs) to finance and implement large-scale renewable projects.
3. **Strengthen Climate Resilience**:
	* Establish early-warning systems for droughts, floods, and heatwaves using existing weather monitoring networks.
	* Develop climate-resilient agricultural practices and promote the adoption of climate-smart technologies like drought-tolerant crops.
	* Invest in disaster preparedness and response infrastructure to mitigate the impact of extreme events.

### Implementation Considerations

These interventions require careful planning and coordination. For instance, implementing CLTS programs will necessitate community engagement and training. Similarly, renewable energy projects need robust grid integration plans to ensure reliability for off-grid communities. Additionally, climate adaptation measures must be integrated into existing development frameworks to maximize impact. The initial costs of these initiatives are significant but can be offset through long-term benefits such as improved health outcomes and economic growth.

### Conclusion

Addressing water security, clean energy access, and climate resilience in Sub-Saharan Africa is crucial for sustainable development. By focusing on evidence-based interventions that prioritize community engagement, renewable energy adoption, and climate adaptation measures, the region can achieve significant progress towards achieving SDG 6 while aligning with global commitments like the Paris Agreement.
