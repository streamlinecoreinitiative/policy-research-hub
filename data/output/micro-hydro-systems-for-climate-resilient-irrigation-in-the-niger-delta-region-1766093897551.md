**Micro-Hydro Systems: An Innovative Solution**

Micro-hydro systems harness the energy of flowing water to generate electricity, providing a reliable and sustainable power source for irrigation. These systems are particularly suitable for regions with high rainfall variability, as they can operate during periods of low river flows. Micro-hydro systems offer several benefits:

• **Cost-effectiveness**: Lower upfront costs compared to traditional fossil fuel-based generators
• **Sustainability**: Renewable energy source, reducing greenhouse gas emissions and dependence on non-renewable resources
• **Reliability**: Consistent power supply, minimizing downtime and maximizing irrigation efficiency
