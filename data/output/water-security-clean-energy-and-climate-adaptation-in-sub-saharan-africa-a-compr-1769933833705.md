## Policy Recommendations

1. **Expand Water Infrastructure**: Invest in sustainable water supply systems (e.g., rainwater harvesting, desalination plants) to ensure safe drinking water access for all communities.
2. **Solar Power Access**: Integrate solar-powered wells and renewable energy mini-grids into rural areas to increase electricity access and reliability.
3. **Energy Efficiency Programs**: Implement policies promoting energy-efficient technologies in households and businesses to reduce reliance on non-renewable sources.
4. **Climate Resilience Initiatives**: Develop early warning systems, disaster preparedness plans, and climate-resilient infrastructure projects (e.g., flood barriers, water storage facilities) to enhance resilience against extreme weather events.
