## Policy Recommendations

1. **Expand Water Harvesting Projects**: Develop and implement large-scale water harvesting facilities (e.g., dams, reservoirs) to increase access to safe drinking water.
2. **Solar-Powered Irrigation Systems**: Deploy solar-powered irrigation pumps in smallholder farms across the region. These systems reduce dependency on fossil fuels and enhance agricultural productivity, contributing to food security (World Bank, 2023).
3. **Enhance Clean Energy Access through Microgrids**: Establish microgrid networks powered by renewable energy sources like solar panels. This approach can significantly increase electricity access in remote areas while reducing reliance on diesel generators.
4. **Early-Warnings Systems and Last-Mile Alerts**: Implement early-warning systems for climate events such as floods and droughts, ensuring timely information reaches vulnerable communities. Develop last-mile alert mechanisms to disseminate critical weather updates directly to households.
