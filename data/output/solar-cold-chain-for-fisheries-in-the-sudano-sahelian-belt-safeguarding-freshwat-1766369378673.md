## Executive Summary

The Sudano-Sahelian belt is highly vulnerable to climate change, characterized by unpredictable rainfall patterns and increasing water scarcity. This brief proposes the adoption of solar-powered cold-chains for fisheries as a solution to safeguard freshwater ecosystems and ensure food security.
