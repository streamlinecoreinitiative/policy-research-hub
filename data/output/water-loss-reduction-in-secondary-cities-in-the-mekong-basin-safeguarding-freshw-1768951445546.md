## Executive Summary

Sub-Saharan Africa faces severe water scarcity, high energy costs, and limited access to clean energy sources. Specifically, governments should focus on developing water storage facilities, desalination plants, and climate-resilient irrigation systems to ensure sustainable water supply (World Bank Open Data).
