This brief highlights the importance of early-warning systems and last-mile alerts in safeguarding these ecosystems. We propose a comprehensive approach integrating agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems for climate-resilient irrigation.

Early-warning systems and last-mile alerts are essential components of a comprehensive approach to safeguarding freshwater ecosystems. These systems can:
