# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Mesoamerican Highlands

## Executive Summary

The Mesoamerican Highlands face severe water scarcity issues exacerbated by climate change impacts on agriculture. This brief proposes an integrated solution combining agroforestry with micro-hydro systems to address these challenges.

## Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Mesoamerican Highlands

Climate change is having a profound impact on agriculture in the Mesoamerican Highlands, leading to severe water scarcity issues. Rising temperatures and changing precipitation patterns reduce crop yields, threatening food security and economic stability for local communities.

### Climate Change Impacts on Agriculture

*   Changes in rainfall patterns and increased evaporation rates due to warmer temperatures
*   Soil degradation and reduced fertility due to erosion and nutrient depletion
*   Increased crop water requirements due to drought stress

Micro-hydro technology offers a sustainable solution for irrigation, reducing reliance on diesel generators and increasing energy efficiency.

### Benefits of Micro-Hydro Technology

*   Renewable energy source with minimal environmental impact
*   High energy conversion efficiency (up to 90%)
*   Reduced operating costs due to lower maintenance requirements
