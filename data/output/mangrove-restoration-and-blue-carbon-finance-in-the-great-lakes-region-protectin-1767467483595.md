# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Mesoamerican Highlands

## Executive Summary

The Mesoamerican Highlands face severe water scarcity and poverty exacerbated by climate change impacts on agriculture. This brief explores the integration of agroforestry and micro-hydro systems as a solution to ensure economic benefits and sustainability.

## Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency

Micro-hydro technology involves the use of small-scale hydroelectric systems to generate energy. This approach reduces reliance on diesel generators and increases energy efficiency. Pilot projects have successfully integrated micro-hydro systems with agroforestry practices, demonstrating potential for scaling up.

## Integration in Climate-Resilient Water Supply

The combination of agroforestry and micro-hydro systems has been piloted in various locations, showcasing its effectiveness in reducing water loss and increasing energy efficiency. Economic analysis highlights the cost-effectiveness of this integrated approach.
