**Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency**

Micro-hydro technology involves small-scale hydroelectric systems that harness energy from flowing water. Benefits include reduced reliance on diesel generators and increased energy efficiency (up to 90%). This technology is particularly suitable for mountainous regions with abundant water resources but limited access to electricity.

**Integration of Agroforestry and Micro-Hydro in Climate-Resilient Water Supply**

Pilot projects have demonstrated the effectiveness of combining agroforestry and micro-hydro systems. These integrated approaches enhance irrigation efficiency, reduce energy consumption, and promote climate resilience. Economic analysis highlights cost-effectiveness and potential for scaling up.
