### Executive Summary

The Lower Mekong delta is highly vulnerable to climate change, experiencing severe heatwaves that exacerbate health issues among its population. ### Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Lower Mekong Delta

The Lower Mekong delta is characterized by erratic rainfall patterns, leading to severe water scarcity. Climate change impacts agriculture, making traditional irrigation methods energy-intensive and costly. Smallholder farmers are disproportionately affected, exacerbating poverty levels. The region's unique geography makes it prone to heatwaves.
