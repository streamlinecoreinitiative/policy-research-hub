**Executive Summary**
Sub-Saharan Africa faces significant challenges in ensuring water security and clean energy access. To address this, we recommend investing in infrastructure projects like boreholes and solar-powered systems, promoting renewable energy adoption, and integrating climate adaptation strategies into existing policies.

**Context & Challenge**
Sub-Saharan Africa is particularly vulnerable to climate change due to its geography and limited resources. The region is projected to experience more frequent droughts and floods due to changing weather patterns, putting immense pressure on already strained water supplies and electricity grids.
