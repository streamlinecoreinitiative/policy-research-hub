**Solar Cold-Chains for Fisheries in the Mekong Basin: Enhancing Resilience and Reducing Disaster Losses**

Executive Summary:

The Mekong Basin is home to over 60 million people, with millions relying on fisheries as a primary source of income and food security. Climate change and extreme weather events pose significant threats to these communities, resulting in devastating losses and displacement. This brief proposes an innovative solution: integrating solar-powered cold-chains into the fisheries sector to enhance resilience.
