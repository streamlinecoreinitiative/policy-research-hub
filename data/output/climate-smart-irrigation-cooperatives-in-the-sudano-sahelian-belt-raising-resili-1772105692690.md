## Policy Recommendations

### Water Security

1. **Invest in large-scale water storage facilities** like dams and reservoirs to store excess water during rainy seasons.
2. **Promote small-scale rainwater harvesting systems** and groundwater recharge initiatives to improve local water supply resilience.
3. **Implement community-based water management programs** to enhance equitable access to safe drinking water.

### Clean Energy

1. **Increase subsidies for solar energy projects** in rural areas, focusing on microgrids that can provide reliable electricity to remote communities.
2. **Encourage private sector investment** through tax incentives and public-private partnerships to promote renewable energy development.
3. **Develop comprehensive training programs** to upskill technicians needed to install and maintain clean energy systems.

### Climate Adaptation

1. **Establish early warning systems** for droughts and floods, using satellite imagery and other technological tools to provide timely alerts.
2. **Implement climate-resilient agricultural practices** such as crop diversification, improved irrigation techniques, and soil conservation measures.
3. **Provide financial support** through grants and loans for adaptation projects that reduce vulnerability to climate change impacts.
