*   **Blue carbon finance**: This approach involves financing mangrove restoration and conservation efforts through the sale of carbon credits (Source: World Bank)

## Policy Recommendations

To address the urgent need for environmental resilience in the Lower Mekong delta, we recommend:

1.  **Prioritize mangrove restoration efforts**: Governments should allocate dedicated funds towards restoring and conserving mangrove forests through blue carbon finance mechanisms.
2.  **Promote sustainable land-use practices**: Encourage local communities to adopt sustainable land-use practices that prioritize mangrove conservation and restoration.
3.  **Enhance climate resilience**: Invest in early warning systems, climate-resilient infrastructure, and disaster risk reduction measures to protect coastal communities from climate-related disasters.
