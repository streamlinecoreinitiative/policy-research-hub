**Executive Summary**

Climate change exacerbates water scarcity, food insecurity, and economic instability in lower-income countries, particularly in the Horn of Africa region. Erratic rainfall patterns and increased temperatures pose significant threats to livelihoods. This brief highlights the importance of early-warning systems and last-mile alerts in protecting communities from climate-induced shocks.
