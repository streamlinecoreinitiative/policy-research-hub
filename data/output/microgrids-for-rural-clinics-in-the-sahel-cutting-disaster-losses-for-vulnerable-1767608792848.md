## Background: Water Scarcity in the Sahel

The Sahel region is characterized by high temperatures, low rainfall, and limited water resources. Climate change exacerbates these conditions, leading to increased frequency and severity of droughts and floods. Agriculture, a primary source of livelihood for many communities, is severely impacted by water scarcity.

Micro-hydro technology offers a viable solution for efficient irrigation in the Sahel. By harnessing the energy of small-scale hydroelectric systems, micro-hydro can:

* Reduce reliance on diesel generators and increase energy efficiency
* Provide reliable power supply to irrigation systems
* Minimize water loss through evaporation and seepage
