# climate-smart irrigation cooperatives in the Sudano-Sahelian belt: protecting livelihoods under climate stress

**Climate-Smart Irrigation Cooperatives in the Sudano-Sahelian Belt: Protecting Livelihoods under Climate Stress**

### Executive Summary

The Sudano-Sahelian belt is one of the most vulnerable regions to climate change, facing severe water scarcity and poverty. This brief proposes an integrated approach to address these challenges through the establishment of climate-smart irrigation cooperatives that combine agroforestry with micro-hydro systems.

### Current Water Scarcity Challenges in the Sudano-Sahelian Belt

The region is characterized by:

* **Water scarcity**: Limited rainfall and increasing evaporation rates due to rising temperatures.
* **Poverty**: High levels of poverty among rural populations, exacerbating vulnerability to climate-related shocks.
* **Food insecurity**: Limited access to reliable water sources for irrigation, impacting agricultural productivity.

### Agroforestry and Micro-Hydro Systems: A Climate-Resilient Solution

Agroforestry practices have been shown to:

* **Improve soil health**: Enhance water retention and reduce erosion. [verify]
* **Increase biodiversity**: Promote ecosystem services and resilience.
* **Sequester carbon**: Mitigate climate change impacts.

Micro-hydro systems offer:

* **Renewable energy**: Reduced reliance on diesel generators and increased energy efficiency.
* **Water conservation**: Efficient use of available water resources.

### Integration of Agroforestry and Micro-Hydro in Climate-Resilient Water Supply

Pilot projects demonstrate the effectiveness of combining agroforestry with micro-hydro systems, showcasing:

* **Improved irrigation efficiency**: Reduced water loss and increased crop yields.
* **Economic benefits**: Cost-effectiveness and potential for scaling up.

### Policy Recommendations and Future Research Directions

To support the adoption of climate-smart irrigation cooperatives at scale, we recommend:

* **Policy frameworks**: Establishing supportive policies and regulations to encourage investment in agroforestry-micro-hydro systems.
* **Capacity building**: Providing training and technical assistance to communities and stakeholders on integrated water management practices.
* **Monitoring and evaluation**: Regularly assessing the effectiveness of these integrated solutions.

Future research should focus on:

* **Economic impact analysis**: Quantifying the economic benefits of climate-smart irrigation cooperatives. [verify]
* **Community engagement**: Understanding the social and cultural factors influencing community acceptance and adoption of these technologies.

### Actions/Recommendations

1. Establish a task force to develop policy frameworks supporting agroforestry-micro-hydro systems.
2. Provide training and technical assistance to communities and stakeholders on integrated water management practices.
3. Develop a monitoring and evaluation framework to assess the effectiveness of climate-smart irrigation cooperatives.

### Closing Outlook

The integration of agroforestry with micro-hydro systems offers a promising solution for addressing water scarcity and poverty in the Sudano-Sahelian belt. By prioritizing this approach, communities can improve their resilience to climate stress while ensuring economic benefits and sustainability.
