## Context & Challenge

The Central America dry corridor is one of the most vulnerable regions to climate change, with frequent droughts affecting agricultural production and food security. Urban areas are particularly susceptible to heat-related stress due to high population densities, inadequate infrastructure, and limited green spaces (World Bank, 2022). The region's economy relies heavily on agriculture, making it essential to address these challenges to ensure sustainable development.
