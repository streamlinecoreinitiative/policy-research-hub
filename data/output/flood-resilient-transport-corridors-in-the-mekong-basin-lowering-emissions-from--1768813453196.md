Flood-resilient transport corridors in the Mekong Basin can be achieved through the integration of sustainable infrastructure development with emission reduction strategies. This approach involves:

*   **Micro-hydro systems**: Providing renewable energy solutions for irrigation, reducing dependence on fossil fuels and lowering greenhouse gas emissions.
*   **Agroforestry practices**: Enhancing soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration, while improving water retention and reducing erosion.
*   **Climate-resilient infrastructure design**: Incorporating flood-resistant materials and designs to minimize damage from extreme weather events.
