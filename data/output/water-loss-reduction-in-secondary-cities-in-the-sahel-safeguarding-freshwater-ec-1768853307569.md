### Policy Recommendations

To address water security and climate resilience, policymakers should:

* **Invest in Climate-Smart Irrigation**: Implement agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems that provide sustainable irrigation solutions (UN-Water/WHO, 2022). These systems can help increase agricultural productivity while reducing reliance on traditional energy sources.
* **Promote Renewable Energy Integration**: Encourage the integration of renewable energy into national power grids through grants and subsidies for clean cooking transition initiatives. This will reduce greenhouse gas emissions and improve access to electricity (World Bank, 2023).
* **Enhance Early Warning Systems**: Develop early-warning systems that provide last-mile alerts for vulnerable groups affected by climate-related disasters. These measures can help minimize disaster losses and ensure better health outcomes during heatwaves.
