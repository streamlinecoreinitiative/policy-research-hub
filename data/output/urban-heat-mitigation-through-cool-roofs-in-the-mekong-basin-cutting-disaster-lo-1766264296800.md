### Executive Summary

The Mekong Basin is highly vulnerable to climate change, experiencing frequent and intense heatwaves that exacerbate urban heat islands. Integrating cool roofs into urban planning can mitigate these effects, particularly among low-income households and small-scale farmers. Governments and development partners can support the adoption of cool roof technologies by leveraging existing infrastructure and community-led initiatives.
