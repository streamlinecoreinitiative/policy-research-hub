## Addressing Water Scarcity in the Andean Highlands

### Context of Water Scarcity

Historical droughts have plagued the Andean highlands, with current challenges including limited access to reliable irrigation systems and economic instability due to reduced crop yields. The region's unique geography and climate make it particularly vulnerable to water scarcity.

### Current Challenges Faced by Farmers and Communities

Farmers in the Andean highlands rely heavily on traditional methods such as surface water and rain-fed systems, which are often inefficient and unsustainable. Economic pressures exacerbate existing challenges, making it difficult for farmers to adapt to changing weather patterns.
