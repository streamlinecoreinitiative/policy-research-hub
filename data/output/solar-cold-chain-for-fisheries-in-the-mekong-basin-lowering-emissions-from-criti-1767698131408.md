# Solar Cold-Chain Solutions for Fisheries in the Mekong Basin: Reducing Emissions from Critical Sectors

## Executive Summary

The Mekong Basin's fisheries are critical to global food security, providing sustenance and livelihoods for millions of people. However, climate change, water scarcity, and inefficient energy use threaten their sustainability. This brief explores the potential of solar-powered cold-chain systems in reducing emissions from these sectors.

## Introduction

The Mekong Basin is a vital region for global food security, with its fisheries providing essential protein sources for millions of people. Climate change and water scarcity are threatening fish populations and livelihoods, while inefficient energy use contributes to greenhouse gas emissions.

## Benefits of Solar Cold-Chain Systems

Solar cold-chain systems offer a promising solution by harnessing solar power to provide reliable cooling for fisheries, reducing the need for diesel generators and minimizing emissions from energy production. These systems also help maintain fish quality and safety, increasing shelf life and value.

### Key Benefits:

• Reduced greenhouse gas emissions from energy production [verify]
• Improved fish quality and safety through consistent refrigeration
• Increased shelf life and value of fish products
• Enhanced livelihoods for communities dependent on fisheries
