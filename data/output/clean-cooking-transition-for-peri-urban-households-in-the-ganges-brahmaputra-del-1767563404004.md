The Ganges-Brahmaputra delta is one of the most densely populated and rapidly urbanizing regions globally, with significant environmental implications. This brief focuses on peri-urban households, which are particularly vulnerable to climate change impacts due to their reliance on traditional cooking methods that contribute to greenhouse gas emissions.
