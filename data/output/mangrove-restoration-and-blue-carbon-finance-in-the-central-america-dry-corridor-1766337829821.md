# Mangrove Restoration and Blue Carbon Finance in the Central America Dry Corridor: Cutting Disaster Losses for Vulnerable Groups

## Executive Summary

The Central America dry corridor is highly vulnerable to climate change, characterized by frequent droughts, floods, and landslides. The region's mangrove forests play a critical role in protecting coastal communities from these disasters, but their degradation due to deforestation and pollution poses significant risks. This brief explores the potential for mangrove restoration and blue carbon finance as a solution to reduce disaster losses and promote sustainable development.
