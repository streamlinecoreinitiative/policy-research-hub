## Policy Recommendations

To enhance water security, clean energy access, and climate resilience in Sub-Saharan Africa:

- **Improved Water Supply Systems**: Invest in rainwater harvesting systems for rural areas to increase access to safe drinking water.
- **Renewable Energy Initiatives**: Expand electrification programs with renewable sources such as solar power. This will not only improve energy access but also reduce reliance on fossil fuels, lowering greenhouse gas emissions and improving climate resilience.
- **Early Warning Systems**: Develop early warning systems for floods and droughts to better prepare communities and minimize losses.
