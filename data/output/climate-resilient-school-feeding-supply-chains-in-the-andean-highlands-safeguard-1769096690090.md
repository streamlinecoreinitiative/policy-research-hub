## Policy Recommendations

1. **Enhanced Water Supply Solutions:**
	* Invest in desalination plants, groundwater exploration, and rainwater harvesting systems to increase access to safe drinking water.
	* Implement wastewater treatment facilities to ensure cleaner sources of potable water.
2. **Renewable Energy Integration:**
	* Prioritize the installation of micro-hydropower stations, solar panels, and wind turbines for off-grid communities.
	* Develop comprehensive renewable energy policies that align with national development plans and integrate them into existing infrastructure projects.
3. **Climate Resilience Measures:**
	* Implement early warning systems for droughts and floods to mitigate impacts on water security and power supply.
	* Encourage sustainable agricultural practices such as agroforestry and soil conservation techniques to enhance resilience.
