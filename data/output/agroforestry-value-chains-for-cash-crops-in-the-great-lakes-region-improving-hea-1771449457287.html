Bridging Water Security, Clean Energy Access, and Climate Resilience in Sub-Saharan Africa

Executive Summary

To address the pressing challenges of water security, clean energy access, and climate resilience in Sub-Saharan Africa, we recommend implementing community-based watershed management programs to improve water quality and availability. This initiative should be complemented by micro-insurance schemes for drought risk mitigation and investments in solar-powered desalination plants to boost water security.

    Sub-Saharan Africa is one of the most vulnerable regions globally concerning access to clean water.
