## Policy Recommendations

1. **Implement Community Water Wells**: Invest in constructing and maintaining community water wells that use gravity-fed systems, reducing reliance on electricity. This approach has been successful in other regions, such as the UN-Water/WHO's efforts to improve access to safe drinking water.
2. **Promote Solar-Powered Electrification Programs**: Launch initiatives to electrify rural areas using solar panels for energy generation and storage. The World Bank Open Data provides evidence of the effectiveness of similar programs worldwide.
3. **Increase Rural Renewable Energy Adoption**: Encourage the use of small-scale renewable energy systems like biogas, wind turbines, or solar home systems in remote communities. This approach has been successful in increasing access to electricity in rural areas (UN SDG Report 2023).

## Implementation Considerations

To implement these recommendations effectively:

* Collaborate with local governments and NGOs for community engagement and project management.
* Allocate resources to build infrastructure, including wells and solar panels, based on cost estimates from similar projects in Africa (World Bank Open Data).
* Address potential barriers by integrating climate adaptation strategies into all programs.
