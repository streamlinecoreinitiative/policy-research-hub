# Flood-Resilient Transport Corridors in the Mekong Basin: Enhancing Disaster Resilience for Vulnerable Groups

## Executive Summary

The Mekong River Basin is highly vulnerable to climate change, experiencing frequent flooding and droughts that affect millions of people. Constructing flood-resilient transport corridors can mitigate these impacts by providing safe transportation routes during extreme weather events.
