1. **Develop early-warning systems**: Establish a network of sensors and monitoring stations to provide real-time data on water levels, precipitation, and other critical factors
2. **Implement last-mile alerts**: Develop mobile apps or SMS-based systems to disseminate timely information to farmers and rural communities about water availability, weather forecasts, and other critical updates
3. **Promote agroforestry practices**: Provide training and technical assistance to farmers to adopt integrated agroforestry systems that optimize water use and reduce waste
4. **Invest in micro-hydro technology**: Support the development of micro-hydro systems that provide sustainable energy for irrigation pumps and other agricultural needs
