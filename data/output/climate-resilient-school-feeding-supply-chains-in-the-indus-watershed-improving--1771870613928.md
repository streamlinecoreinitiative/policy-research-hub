1. **Community-Led Water Projects**: Invest in community-driven water projects that prioritize safe drinking water access. Support microfinance initiatives to fund boreholes, rainwater harvesting systems, and other sustainable solutions.
2. **Solar-Powered Clean Energy Solutions**: Leverage solar technology for decentralized clean energy generation in rural areas. Provide subsidies or grants for households and small businesses to adopt solar-powered appliances like lights, refrigerators, and cookstoves.
3. **Climate Adaptation Strategies**: Develop comprehensive climate adaptation plans that integrate early warning systems, disaster risk reduction measures, and resilient infrastructure designs.
