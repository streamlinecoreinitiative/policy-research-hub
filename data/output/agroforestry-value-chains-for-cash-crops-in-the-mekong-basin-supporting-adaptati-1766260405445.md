## Current Irrigation Practices and Challenges

Traditional irrigation systems have several limitations:

*   **Energy Intensity**: Diesel generators are often used for pumping water, leading to high energy costs.
*   **Water Loss**: Evaporation and seepage reduce the efficiency of traditional irrigation methods.
*   **Cost**: High upfront costs and maintenance expenses make these systems unaffordable for many smallholder farmers.

## Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency

Micro-hydro technology is a small-scale hydroelectric system that harnesses energy from water flow. Its benefits include:

*   **Renewable Energy**: Micro-hydro systems provide clean and renewable energy for irrigation.
*   **Energy Efficiency**: These systems are more efficient than diesel generators, reducing energy costs.
*   **Scalability**: Micro-hydro technology can be scaled up or down depending on water flow and energy needs.
