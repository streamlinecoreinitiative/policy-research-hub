**Agroforestry Systems Overview**

Agroforestry systems integrate trees into farming landscapes, promoting soil health, reducing erosion, and conserving water. By mimicking natural ecosystems, agroforestry systems can improve biodiversity, increase crop yields, and enhance resilience to climate-related stresses (OECD Environment Directorate, 2019). Key benefits of agroforestry systems include:

• Improved soil fertility through organic matter addition
• Reduced soil erosion through tree root stabilization
• Enhanced water retention through canopy cover
• Increased biodiversity through habitat creation
