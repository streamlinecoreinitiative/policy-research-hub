## Policy Recommendations

1. **Enhance Water Infrastructure**: Invest in rainwater harvesting, desalination plants, and wastewater treatment facilities to improve access to safe drinking water.
	* Allocate dedicated funding streams for sustainable water management projects (World Bank, 2024).
2. **Promote Renewable Energy Deployment**: Encourage solar power generation through grants and subsidies, particularly in off-grid areas where grid connectivity is limited (World Bank, 2021).
3. **Climate Adaptation Programs**: Implement climate-smart agriculture initiatives that incorporate drought-resistant crop varieties and improved irrigation systems.
