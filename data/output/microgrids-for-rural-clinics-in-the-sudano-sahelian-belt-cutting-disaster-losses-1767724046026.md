**Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Ethiopian Highlands**

**Executive Summary**

The Ethiopian Highlands face severe water scarcity due to climate change impacts, affecting agricultural productivity and rural livelihoods. This brief proposes integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems as a solution to address this issue. Agroforestry improves soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration, while micro-hydro systems provide sustainable and renewable energy for irrigation.
