Executive Summary To address the pressing issues of water security, clean energy access, and climate adaptation in Sub-Saharan Africa (SSA), we recommend a multi-faceted approach that prioritizes infrastructure development, community engagement, and technology transfer. Specifically, governments should allocate significant portions of existing development budgets towards large-scale water treatment facilities, renewable energy projects, and early warning systems for droughts and floods.
