**Executive Summary**

The Lower Mekong delta is vulnerable to climate change, facing severe water scarcity, erratic rainfall patterns, and increased evaporation from wetlands. This brief outlines a comprehensive plan to address these challenges by integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems.

**Introduction**

Traditional irrigation systems are energy-intensive and costly, straining economic challenges faced by smallholder farmers in vulnerable communities.
