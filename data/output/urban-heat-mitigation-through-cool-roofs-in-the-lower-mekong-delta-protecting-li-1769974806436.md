To mitigate the effects of climate stress on urban areas in the Lower Mekong Delta, we recommend implementing cool roof technologies to reduce urban heat islands. 1. **Implement Cool Roof Technologies**: Provide incentives for building owners and governments to adopt cool roof technologies, particularly in densely populated cities.
