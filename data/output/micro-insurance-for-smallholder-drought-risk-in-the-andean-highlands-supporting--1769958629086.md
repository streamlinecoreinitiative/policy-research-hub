Sub-Saharan Africa faces significant disparities in access to basic services such as clean drinking water and electricity. Climate change is exacerbating these challenges, leading to more frequent droughts and floods that strain already limited resources.

## Policy Recommendations

1. **Enhanced Water Infrastructure Development**:
   - Invest in sustainable water infrastructure projects to increase access to safe drinking water.
   - Implement cost-sharing models with local communities to ensure sustained use of facilities.
   - Source: World Bank Open Data
2. **Promoting Renewable Energy Adoption**:
   - Encourage the development and integration of renewable energy sources like solar, wind, and hydropower in rural areas.

3. **Climate Resilience Measures**:
   - Develop early warning systems for climate-related disasters with a focus on droughts and floods.
