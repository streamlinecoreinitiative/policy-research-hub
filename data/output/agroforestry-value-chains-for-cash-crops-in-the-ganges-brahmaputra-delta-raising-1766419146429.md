# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Mesoamerican Highlands

## Executive Summary

The Mesoamerican Highlands face severe water scarcity and poverty exacerbated by climate change impacts on agriculture. This brief explores the integration of agroforestry and micro-hydro systems to address these challenges.

Agroforestry enhances soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration while improving water retention and reducing erosion. Micro-hydro technology increases energy efficiency, reduces reliance on diesel generators, and provides a sustainable source of power for irrigation. By combining these approaches, communities can achieve climate-resilient water supply, economic stability, and food security.

## Environmental Resilience in Lower-Income Countries: A Focus on Water Security

### Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Mesoamerican Highlands

Climate change has significant impacts on agriculture in the Mesoamerican Highlands. Rising temperatures, altered precipitation patterns, and increased frequency of extreme weather events threaten crop yields and food security.

*   Climate data from NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) reveals changes in rainfall patterns over the past decade.
*   The region's water scarcity is further exacerbated by inefficient irrigation systems, resulting in significant water loss due to evaporation and seepage.

### Current Irrigation Practices and Challenges

Existing irrigation systems in the Mesoamerican Highlands include boreholes and canals. However, these methods are often plagued by inefficiencies:

*   Water loss percentages from these systems range from 30% evaporation to 5% seepage.
*   The reliance on diesel generators for power further contributes to environmental degradation.

### Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency

Micro-hydro technology offers a sustainable solution for irrigation:

*   Small-scale hydroelectric systems harness energy from rivers and streams
*   Reduced reliance on diesel generators decreases greenhouse gas emissions and environmental degradation
*   Increased energy efficiency provides a reliable source of power for irrigation
