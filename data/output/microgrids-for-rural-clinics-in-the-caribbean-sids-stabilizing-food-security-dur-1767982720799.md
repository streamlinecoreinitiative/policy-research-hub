# Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems for Climate-Resilient Irrigation in the Ethiopian Highlands

## Executive Summary

The Ethiopian Highlands face severe water scarcity due to climate change impacts, affecting agriculture and food security. This brief proposes integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems as a solution to address this issue. Agroforestry improves soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration, while micro-hydro systems provide sustainable and renewable energy solutions for irrigation efficiency.
