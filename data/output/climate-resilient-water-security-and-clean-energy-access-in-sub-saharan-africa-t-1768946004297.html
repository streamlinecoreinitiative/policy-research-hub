    Policy Recommendations

    To address these challenges and improve resilience:

    1. Microgrids for Rural Clinics: Install solar-powered water wells in underserved rural areas to ensure continuous access to clean water. These microgrid systems can provide sustainable, reliable water supply without the need for grid connections.

  • Climate-Resilient School Feeding Supply Chains: Develop climate-resilient school feeding programs that integrate drought-resistant crops and energy-efficient cooking methods to reduce dependence on imported fuels.
  • Nature-Based Solutions for Landslide Risk: Implement nature-based solutions such as tree planting, soil conservation practices, and erosion control measures in landslide-prone areas. These can help stabilize slopes, reducing the risk of landslides and improving water infiltration.
