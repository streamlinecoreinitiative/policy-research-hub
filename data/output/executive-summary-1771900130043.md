To address these challenges, governments and international organizations should prioritize the following actionable steps:

1. **Increase Access to Safe Drinking Water:**
	* Invest in infrastructure such as boreholes and rainwater harvesting systems (UN-Water/WHO, 2022)
	* Implement community-led initiatives that promote hygiene practices
2. **Promote Renewable Energy Integration:**
	* Encourage the use of solar-powered pumps and other renewable energy technologies for water extraction
	* Support decentralized solar power projects in rural areas with a total investment of $500 million (World Bank, 2024)
3. **Climate Adaptation Measures:**
	* Develop early warning systems for droughts and floods to help communities prepare and mitigate risks (NOAA climate data)
