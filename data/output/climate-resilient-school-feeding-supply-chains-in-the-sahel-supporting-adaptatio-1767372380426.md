**Executive Summary**

The Sahel region is highly vulnerable to climate change, with frequent droughts and water scarcity affecting agricultural productivity and food security. The school feeding program faces challenges due to limited access to clean energy, water, and sustainable irrigation systems. This brief proposes an integrated approach combining agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems with climate-resilient school feeding supply chains to support adaptation targets in NDCs.
