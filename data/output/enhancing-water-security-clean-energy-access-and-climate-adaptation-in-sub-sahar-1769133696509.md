1. **Strengthen Existing Water Management Systems**: Improve access to safe drinking water through cost-effective solutions like rainwater harvesting and borehole drilling.
2. **Increase Investment in Clean Energy Infrastructure**: Invest in solar-powered desalination plants and renewable energy projects. Provide subsidies or grants for households and small businesses to adopt these technologies.
3. **Promote Climate-Resilient Agricultural Practices**: Implement agroforestry value chains, which can improve water management and reduce vulnerability to droughts and other climate-related shocks.
