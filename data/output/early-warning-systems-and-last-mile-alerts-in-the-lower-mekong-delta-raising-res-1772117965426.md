2. **Develop Last-Mile Alerts**: Establish a network of community-based alert systems, using mobile phones, radios, or other low-cost technologies to disseminate critical information to communities.
3. **Integrate with Climate-Resilient Infrastructure**: Ensure that EWS and last-mile alerts are integrated with climate-resilient infrastructure, such as flood-resistant homes and water management systems.
