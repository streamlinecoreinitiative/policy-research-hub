## Context & Challenge

Sub-Saharan Africa is highly vulnerable to climate change due to its high dependence on natural resources and low adaptive capacity. Water scarcity and limited access to clean energy are major challenges, exacerbating food insecurity and health issues (World Bank, 2023). The region lacks sufficient infrastructure for water supply, sanitation, and electricity generation, making it difficult to meet basic needs and support economic growth.
