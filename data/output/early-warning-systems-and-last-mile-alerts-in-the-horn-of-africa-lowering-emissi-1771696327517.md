## Policy Recommendations

To address these challenges, we recommend:

1. **Invest in Renewable Energy**: Expand renewable energy projects to increase access to electricity by at least 10% within the next five years (World Bank, 2024). This can be achieved through government-led initiatives that incentivize private sector participation and community-based solar power installations.
2. **Strengthen Water Management**: Implement water conservation measures such as rainwater harvesting and wastewater treatment plants in urban centers to ensure sustainable access to clean drinking water. Additionally, promote drip irrigation systems for agriculture to reduce water usage without compromising productivity.
3. **Enhance Climate Resilience**: Develop early warning systems for droughts and floods using satellite imagery and ground-based sensors. This will help communities prepare more effectively and minimize losses during extreme weather events.
