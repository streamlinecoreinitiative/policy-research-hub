**Climate-Smart Irrigation Cooperatives in the Mekong Basin: Supporting Adaptation Targets in NDCs**

**Executive Summary**
The Mekong Basin is a critical region for agricultural production, facing significant water security challenges due to climate change. This brief explores the potential of integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems as a solution to enhance both water security and renewable energy use.

**I. Introduction to Water Scarcity in the Mekong Basin**

The Mekong Basin is a vital region for agriculture, supporting millions of people through its rich water resources. Climate change has led to increased variability in rainfall patterns, resulting in severe water scarcity and affecting agricultural productivity.

**II. Current Irrigation Practices and Challenges**

Existing irrigation systems in the Mekong Basin often rely on boreholes or canals, which are prone to inefficiencies that waste precious water resources and increase energy consumption.
