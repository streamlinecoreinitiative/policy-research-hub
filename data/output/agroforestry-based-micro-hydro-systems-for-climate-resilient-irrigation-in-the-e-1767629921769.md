Executive Summary:

The Ethiopian Highlands face severe water scarcity due to climate change impacts on agriculture. Traditional irrigation methods are energy-intensive and costly, while micro-hydro systems provide sustainable and renewable energy. This brief proposes integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems to address water scarcity and promote climate resilience.
