## Environmental Resilience in Lower-Income Countries: Key Challenges

### Water Security

* Severe water scarcity affects agriculture, leading to crop failures and reduced yields.
* Erratic rainfall patterns exacerbate the issue, making irrigation a significant challenge.
* Existing irrigation systems often rely on diesel generators or boreholes, which are energy-intensive and inefficient.

### Clean Energy

* Renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, may not be feasible due to high upfront costs and intermittency concerns.
* Micro-hydro technology offers a reliable and efficient solution for generating electricity in rural areas.

### Climate Adaptation

* Climate-related disasters, such as floods and droughts, have devastating impacts on vulnerable communities.
* Agroforestry-based micro-hydro systems can help mitigate these effects by providing climate-resilient irrigation and reducing soil erosion.
