# **Water Loss Reduction in Secondary Cities of the Sahel: A Climate-Resilient Approach**

## Executive Summary

The Sahel region faces severe water scarcity exacerbated by climate change, posing significant challenges to vulnerable communities. This brief examines the potential of integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems as a solution to reduce water loss and promote resilience in secondary cities.

## Water Scarcity Challenges in Secondary Cities of the Sahel

### Overview of Climate Change Impacts on Agriculture

Climate change is altering rainfall patterns, leading to droughts and floods that severely impact agricultural productivity. The Sahel region has experienced a decline in rainfall over the past decade [verify], exacerbating water scarcity issues.

### Importance of Resilient Irrigation Methods for Food Security and Economic Stability

Resilient irrigation methods are crucial for ensuring food security and economic stability in vulnerable communities. However, existing irrigation systems often suffer from high water loss percentages due to evaporation and seepage.

## Agroforestry-Based Micro-Hydro Systems: A Solution to Water Scarcity

### Benefits of Agroforestry for Soil Health, Biodiversity, and Carbon Sequestration

Agroforestry practices improve soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration. Case studies from the Mesoamerican Highlands demonstrate how agroforestry improves water retention and reduces erosion (Mexican Ministry of Agriculture Publication).

### Micro-Hydro Systems for Irrigation Efficiency

Micro-hydro technology offers a sustainable solution to irrigation efficiency by reducing reliance on diesel generators.

## Integration of Agroforestry and Micro-Hydro in Climate-Resilient Water Supply

### Pilot Projects Showcasing the Combined Use of Agroforestry and Micro-Hydro

Pilot projects have demonstrated the effectiveness of integrating agroforestry with micro-hydro systems to reduce water loss and promote resilience. Economic analysis highlights the cost-effectiveness and potential for scaling up these solutions.

## Policy Recommendations and Future Research Directions

### Policy Recommendations to Support Adoption at Scale

To support adoption at scale, policy recommendations include:

* Providing incentives for community-led agroforestry initiatives
* Investing in micro-hydro infrastructure development
* Promoting renewable energy consumption through tax breaks or subsidies

### Suggested Areas for Further Research

Further research is needed on the economic impact of these integrated solutions and community engagement strategies to ensure successful adoption.

## Actions/Recommendations

1. **Invest in Community-Led Agroforestry Initiatives**: Provide incentives for communities to adopt agroforestry practices.
2. **Develop Micro-Hydro Infrastructure**: Invest in micro-hydro infrastructure development to increase energy efficiency.
3. **Promote Renewable Energy Consumption**: Offer tax breaks or subsidies to promote renewable energy consumption.

## Closing Outlook

The integration of agroforestry with micro-hydro systems offers a promising solution to water scarcity challenges in secondary cities of the Sahel. By addressing immediate water scarcity issues and contributing to broader sustainable development goals, this approach can help reduce disaster losses for vulnerable groups and promote climate resilience in the region.
