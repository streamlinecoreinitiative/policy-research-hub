# Solar-Powered Cold-Chain Systems for Fisheries in Central America: Enhancing Food Security During Droughts

## Executive Summary

The Central America dry corridor is highly vulnerable to climate change, experiencing severe droughts and water scarcity that impact food security and livelihoods. This brief explores the potential of solar-powered cold-chain systems for fisheries in this region, highlighting their role in stabilizing food security during droughts.

* Water scarcity has significant impacts on food security, economic stability, and human well-being.
