## Introduction to Water Scarcity

The Central African Rainforest is one of the most biodiverse regions on Earth, supporting a wide range of plant and animal species. However, it faces severe water scarcity due to climate change, including erratic rainfall patterns and increased evaporation from vast wetlands. This has significant impacts on agriculture in the region.

### Overview of Climate Change Impacts

- **Temperature Increase:** The Central African Rainforest is warming at a rate faster than the global average.
- **Rainfall Variability:** Erratic rainfall events are becoming more frequent, making it difficult for farmers to predict when water will be available for irrigation.
- **Water Stress:** The combination of increased evaporation and reduced rainfall is resulting in severe water stress for crops.

### Importance of Resilient Irrigation Methods

Resilient irrigation methods are crucial for maintaining food security and economic stability in the face of climate change. Traditional irrigation systems rely on diesel generators, which contribute to greenhouse gas emissions and increase energy costs.
