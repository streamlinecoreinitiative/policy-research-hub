# Early-Warning Systems and Last-Mile Alerts in the Caribbean SIDS: Improving Health Outcomes in Heatwaves

## Executive Summary

The Caribbean Small Island Developing States (SIDS) are highly vulnerable to heat-related health impacts due to their geographical location, climate change, and limited resources. The lack of effective early-warning systems and last-mile alerts exacerbates this issue, putting the lives of millions at risk.
