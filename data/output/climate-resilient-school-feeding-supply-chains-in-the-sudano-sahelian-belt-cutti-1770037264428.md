These figures underscore a need for urgent and targeted interventions.

## Policy Recommendations

1. **Enhanced Water Supply Systems**:
   - Invest in existing boreholes and wells for improved water supply systems.
   - Implement rainwater harvesting ponds and other catchment management projects to augment groundwater resources.
   - Source: World Bank (2024), UN-Water/WHO (2022).

2. **Promoting Renewable Energy**:
   - Support microgrids powered by solar, wind, or hydroelectric sources for rural clinics and communities lacking grid access.
   - Encourage community-based renewable energy projects that focus on decentralized systems.
   - Source: World Bank (2023), UN-Water/WHO (2022).

3. **Climate Adaptation Strategies**:
   - Implement early-warning systems to mitigate the impacts of climate change, particularly focusing on drought and water scarcity.
   - Develop watershed management programs that integrate indigenous knowledge with modern practices for sustainable land use and water resource management.
   - Source: World Bank (2024), UN-Water/WHO (2022).

## Implementation Considerations

- **Funding**: Mobilize public-private partnerships to fund these initiatives, leveraging existing grants and loans from international development agencies.
- **Capacity Building**: Train local communities in water management and renewable energy technologies to ensure long-term sustainability.
- **Regulatory Frameworks**: Establish clear regulatory frameworks that incentivize the adoption of sustainable practices and penalize non-compliance.

### Background Information

**Environment**

The natural environment, also known as the physical environment or biophysical environment, refers to all living and non-living components that make up our planet. This includes air, water, land, flora, fauna, and climate.

**Resilience**

Resilience is the ability of a system, community, or individual to withstand or recover from adversity, trauma, or stress. In the context of this policy brief, resilience refers to the capacity of Sub-Saharan Africa's water supply systems, energy infrastructure, and communities to adapt to and mitigate the impacts of climate change.
