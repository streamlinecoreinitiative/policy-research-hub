1. **Invest in Water Infrastructure**: Develop and finance large-scale water storage facilities, particularly in arid regions where rainfall is unpredictable. Implement cost-effective desalination plants for coastal areas.
2. **Renewable Energy Deployment**: Increase investments in wind, solar, and hydroelectric projects to reduce reliance on fossil fuels. Provide subsidies and incentives for small-scale renewable energy systems like biogas digesters for cooking and electricity generation.
3. **Community Engagement**: Encourage local governments and NGOs to engage communities in water management initiatives. Promote sustainable agricultural practices that conserve water resources.
