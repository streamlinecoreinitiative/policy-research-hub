**Section 2: Microgrids for Rural Clinics**

Microgrids are decentralized energy systems that combine renewable energy sources with energy storage systems. They offer several benefits for rural clinics in the Central America dry corridor:

* **Reliability:** Microgrids ensure a reliable supply of electricity, reducing the risk of power outages and equipment damage during natural disasters
* **Sustainability:** Renewable energy sources reduce greenhouse gas emissions and dependence on fossil fuels
* **Cost-Effectiveness:** Microgrids can be designed to meet specific energy needs, reducing costs associated with traditional grid connections
