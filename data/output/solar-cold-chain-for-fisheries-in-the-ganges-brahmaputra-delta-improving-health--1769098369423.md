3. **Enhance Water Infrastructure**: Invest in modernizing existing water infrastructure to ensure it is equipped with renewable energy solutions like solar-powered pumps, thus enhancing efficiency and reliability.

**Conclusion**
To effectively tackle the water security and energy access gaps in Sub-Saharan Africa, concerted efforts are needed from all stakeholders.
