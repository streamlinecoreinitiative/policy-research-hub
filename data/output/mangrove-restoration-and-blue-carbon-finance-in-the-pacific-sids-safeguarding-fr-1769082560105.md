## Policy Recommendations

To bridge the gaps in water security, clean energy, and climate adaptation, we recommend:

1. **Strengthen Water Supply Systems**: Implement water point maintenance programs, expand piped water supply systems, and invest in desalination projects for coastal regions.
2. **Promote Clean Energy**: Encourage solar home systems, rooftop photovoltaic installations, and community-based wind power initiatives.
3. **Enhance Climate Adaptation**: Develop early warning systems for droughts and floods, enhance agricultural practices to withstand climate impacts, and promote green infrastructure like mangrove restoration.
