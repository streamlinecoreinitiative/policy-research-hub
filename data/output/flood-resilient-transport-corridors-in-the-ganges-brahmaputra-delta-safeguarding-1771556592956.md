## Policy Recommendations

1. **Invest in Renewable Energy Projects**: Governments should allocate funds towards renewable energy projects specifically designed to support water security and improve access to electricity.
2. **Develop Public-Private Partnerships (PPP)**: Encourage partnerships between governments, NGOs, and private sector entities to finance and implement these projects.
3. **Promote Technological Innovations**: Support R&D for cost-effective technologies that integrate solar power with water purification processes.
4. **Educate Communities**: Implement community-based education programs to raise awareness about the benefits of renewable energy in water management.

## Implementation Considerations

- **Costs and Funding**: Estimate initial capital costs for projects, seeking grants from international development organizations such as the World Bank or UN agencies.
- **Challenges and Barriers**: Address potential barriers like resistance from communities who may be skeptical about new technologies or concerns over job displacement.
- **Monitoring & Evaluation**: Establish mechanisms to track project progress and effectiveness in ensuring sustainable outcomes.
