### Introduction to Water Scarcity

Climate change significantly affects agriculture in the Ganges-Brahmaputra delta, with erratic rainfall patterns and increased evaporation from vast wetlands impacting water availability for crops. Traditional irrigation methods are energy-intensive and costly, exacerbating economic challenges for smallholder farmers in vulnerable communities.
