## Introduction to Water Scarcity

Climate change has profound implications for agriculture in the Great Lakes region, leading to erratic rainfall patterns and increased evaporation from wetlands. Traditional irrigation methods are often energy-intensive and unsustainable, exacerbating economic challenges for smallholder farmers.
