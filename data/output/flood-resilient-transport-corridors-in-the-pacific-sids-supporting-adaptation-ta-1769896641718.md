1. **Invest in Water Infrastructure**: Develop and finance infrastructure projects such as rainwater harvesting systems, desalination plants, and improved sanitation facilities. Target areas with the lowest access rates.
2. **Promote Renewable Energy**: Encourage investment in solar, wind, and hydro power to meet growing energy demands sustainably. Implement incentives for renewable energy adoption, including subsidies and tax breaks.
3. **Climate Resilient Planning**: Incorporate climate resilience into water management plans and energy infrastructure projects. Conduct risk assessments and build adaptive capacity through early warning systems and disaster preparedness programs.
