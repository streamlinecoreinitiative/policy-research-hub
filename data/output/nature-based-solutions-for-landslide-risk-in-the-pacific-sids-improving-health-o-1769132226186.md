## Policy Recommendations

1. **Water Security Enhancements**:
   - **Expand Water Infrastructure**: Invest in water supply systems such as wells, pipelines, and purification facilities to improve access to safe drinking water.
   - **Promote Rainwater Harvesting**: Implement policies that encourage rainwater harvesting and groundwater recharge projects to enhance local water resources.

2. **Clean Energy Access Expansion**:
   - **Subsidize Solar Panels**: Provide subsidies for solar panel installation in rural areas, making clean energy more affordable and accessible.
   - **Community-Based Renewable Energy Projects**: Support community-led initiatives such as mini-grids and biogas digesters to increase electricity access.

3. **Climate Adaptation Measures**:
   - **Early Warning Systems**: Implement early warning systems for droughts, floods, and other climate-related events using technology like IoT sensors and satellite imagery.
   - **Afforestation Programs**: Launch afforestation programs to enhance natural disaster resilience by increasing green cover and reducing soil erosion.
