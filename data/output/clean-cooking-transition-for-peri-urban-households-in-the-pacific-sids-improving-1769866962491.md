## Policy Recommendations

To address the urgent need for urban heat mitigation in Sub-Saharan Africa, we recommend:

1. **Encourage government investment** through grants or tax incentives for cool roof installations.
2. **Integrate cool roof technology into building codes and zoning regulations**, ensuring widespread adoption.

Addressing potential barriers requires:

* Providing subsidies for materials

Sub-Saharan African cities face significant challenges due to urban heat island effects.
